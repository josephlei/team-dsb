Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential

# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Lack food safety certificate. Provide within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: The following areas observed unclean:
*floor below cook's line
*in between sink basins at 2 compartment sink
*on floor, in hard to reach areas, below large equipment
*fan in upright cooler near entrance door
*light switches for exhaust hood and exhaust lighting
Correct within 72 hours and maintain.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 44b

APPROPRIATE USE OF EQUIPMENT

OBSERVATIONS: Two compartment sink that is directly plumbed to sewer is used as a preparation sink. Immediately cease practice
and use preparation sink that drains in an approved manner into floor sink. Correct immediately. NOTE: approved preparation sink
on site.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not clean their hands in a sink used for food preparation, warewashing, or in a service sink or a
curbed cleaning facility used for the disposal of mop water and similar liquid waste. A handwashing facility shall not be used for purposes
other than handwashing. Food preparation sinks, handwashing lavatories, and warewashing equipment shall not be used for the cleaning
of maintenance tools, the preparation or holding of maintenance materials, or the disposal of mop water and similar liquid wastes. Open-air
barbecue or outdoor wood-burning oven shall be operated on the same premises as, in reasonable proximity to, and in conjunction with, a
permanent food facility that is approved for food preparation. (113953.1 (b-d), 114123, 114143 (a,b), Exception 114125)

# 44e

VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION

OBSERVATIONS: Back door and screen door propped open. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage
of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device
and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food
or utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if
required. (114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Once missing cove base tile on floor below glove station. Correct within 30 days.
