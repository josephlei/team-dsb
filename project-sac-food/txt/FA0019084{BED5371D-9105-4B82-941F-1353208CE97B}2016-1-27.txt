#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Sandwiches and wraps in left-side open merchandiser were measured at 43 to 45 F. Milk and soft cheese in
right-side open merchandiser were measured at 45 to 47 F. PHFs were already moved to the colder left sides of the units. PHFs
were moved into the closed merchandisers beside the units as a temporary corrective measure until the units are enabled to keep
all contents at 41 F or below.
- Director stated that she has already ordered the plastic curtains to cover the fronts of the units.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

NOTES
