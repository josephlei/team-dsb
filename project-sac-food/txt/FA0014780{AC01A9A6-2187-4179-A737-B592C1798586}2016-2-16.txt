Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All

#8

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

OBSERVATIONS: Fried chicken was at 124-129F in hot holding case near self service registers. The time stamp on chicken stated it
came out of the oven at 9:00am. Temperature taken at 1:10 pm. Per manager deli items are time stamped and discarded every four
hours. Corrected by voluntarily discarding chicken during inspectinon.

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be
marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within
the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was
used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Manager's food safety certificate is expired. Provide a new certificate within 60 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Hand sink is blocked by rolling speed rack containing bakery items. Relocate rack so that sink is clear and
accessible at all times. Another fully stocked hand sink is nearby. Corrected during inspection.
Paper towels not dispensing by roll of paper towels were locted on top of dispenser. Keep inside wall mounted dispenser at all
times. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Hand wash sinks in the deli and bakery have hot water that goes to 69-70F. Automatic hand sinks must provide
warm water between 100-108F. Correct within 7 days (REPEAT VIOLATIONA.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
