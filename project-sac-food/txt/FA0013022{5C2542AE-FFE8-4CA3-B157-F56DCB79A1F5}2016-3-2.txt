MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: Sausage and eggs held at buffet measured 99 to 113F.

Note:
In addition to the methods described in the handout for use of time as a public health control, facility is approved to use time as a
public health control as follows: the label of each food item shall indicate that time as a public health control is used and the time
period the item will be held for service each day. The time period shall not exceed 4 hours for any item, and any food remaining after
service shall be discarded. Also, the label shall be retained during each service period.

held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
