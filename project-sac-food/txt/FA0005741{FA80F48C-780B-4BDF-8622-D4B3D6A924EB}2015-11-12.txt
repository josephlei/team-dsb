# 15

FOOD OBTAINED FROM APPROVED SOURCE

OBSERVATIONS: Observed frozen whole chickens and red peppers in plastic bags without labels located in chest freezers near
front door. Operator was unable to produce receipts proving items come from an approved source. Items were removed from sales
floor and are being taken home.
Retain all receipts from all vendors so that facility has proof that all items come from an approved source. A reinspection may be
conducted to follow up on compliance.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be obtained from an approved sources that comply with all applicable laws. Food shall be transported in
an approved manner. Food stored or prepared in a private home shall not be used or offered for sale in a food facility. (113982, 114021,
114023, 114024, 114027, 114029, 114031, 114041)

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

OBSERVATIONS: Facility is using Time as a Public Health Control (TPHC) for items delivered by The Chic Express. At 9:35 am,
TPHC log indicated that fried bananas were delivered and taken out of temperature control at 9:40 am.
According to routine inspection report dated 8/26/14 regarding TPHC:
"This procedure can only be used for places that manufacture the food and the retail market doesn't have the permit to sell
unpackaged, potentially hazardous foods."
According to routine inspection report dated 9/15/14 regarding TPHC:
"Lo Market is only permitted to keep food either under refrigeration at/below 41F or in a hot holding case at 135F or above. Facility
is not approved to use Time as a Public Health Control (TPHC) or logs. This is only approved if faciltiy makes the food at that
location so time it was prepared and removed from temperature control can be documented. Potentially hazardous foods can not sit
out at room temperature."
Facility must discontinue using TPHC. All items delivered must immediately be placed in refrigeration or hot holding as described
above. Future repeat violations will result in all affected food being discarded, regardless of how long it is said to have been left
out. Relocate items to refrigeration immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be
marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within
the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was
used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 29

IDENTIFICATION AND STORAGE OF TOXIC SUBSTANCES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed pan of pellet rodentcide under shelf located on sales floor. Use approved pest control methods only.
Facility may use glue or snap traps, or other methods that trap vermin only. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: All poisonous substances, detergents, sanitizers, and cleaning compounds shall be stored to prevent contamination
of food, equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles. Only pesticides that are necessary and specifically approved for use in a food
facility may be used. Containers of poisonous or toxic materials shall have a legible manufacturer's label or if taken from bulk supplies shall
be clearly and individually identified with the common name of the material. (114254, 114254.1, 114254.2)

FOOD PRESENTATION AND LABELING

OBSERVATIONS: Observed bags of donuts delivered by Sweet Dozen (5207 Madison Ave., Ste. E) without labels. Labels are also
missing address of manufacturer. Label all times immediately. Provide new labels with facility address within 7 days.
