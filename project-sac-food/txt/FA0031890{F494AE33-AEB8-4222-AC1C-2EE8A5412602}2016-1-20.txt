Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential
to cause foodborne illness. All major violations must be corrected immediately. Non-compliance may warrant immediate closure of the food facility.

# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Food safety certificate is still just a copy. Provide original document and maintain on site within 7 days. Failure to
have an original certificate at the next routine inspection may result in a compliance conference charged approximately $400.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Tomatoes (57F) in the condiment tray and chili (102F) in the dispenser were out of proper holding temperature.
Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHF) at proper holding temperature. Both items were voluntarily discarded at the time of
inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: About 15 rodent droppings were observed on the left side of the bottled tea refrigerator, behind the coffee/milk
refrigerator and to the left of the energy drink refrigerator. Clean and sanitize these areas immediately. Provide traps as deemed
necessary.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Counter below the bread toaster is in need of cleaning. Clean within 24 hours.
Floor under the refrigerator in front of the walk-in door is in need of cleaning. Clean within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Quat ammonia test strips for spray bottle were not available. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used.
Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical
warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)

# 40

WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE

OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer within spray bottle was observed at 0ppm quat ammonia. Maintain solution at proper concentration at all
times. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water
with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact
surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Leak is still observed at the base of the faucet. Repair leak on the faucet within 7 days. REPEAT VIOLATION.
Leak observed on the piping below the left compartment of the warewash sink. Repair to eliminate leak within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Jacket and purse were stored above food related items in the back area rack. Store all personal items under or
away from all food and food related items at all times. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers
or other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Sign requiring employees to wash hands after restroom use is not available. Post the provided sign within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas.
Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating
that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
