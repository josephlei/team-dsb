HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Cut tomato in salad bowl on counter to the left of prep refrigerator was at 58F. Once tomato has been cut keep
inside prep refrigerator either inside insert of cold top or in reach in portion of refrigerator. Corrected during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED

OBSERVATIONS: Food debris at top part of meat slicer. It is in the storage position and not in active use. Ensure slicer is stored
clean with no food debris. Parts should be broken down and it should go through the wash, rinse, sanitize and air dry steps. Correct
today (REPEAT VIOLATION).
Slime like accumulation on deflector plate inside ice machine. Clean and sanitize within 2 days (REPEAT VIOLATION).
Buildup on outside of food storage containers under wood prep table. Clean and maintain within 2 days (REPEAT VIOLATION).

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each
use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and
potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred.
Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)

FOOD CONTAINERS LABELED

OBSERVATIONS: Containers of flour under the wood prep table are not labeled. Label with contents within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Containers holding food or food ingredients that are removed from their original packaging shall be identified with the
common name of the food. (114051)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Food accumulation on floor under pizza oven, stove and refrigeration. Clean and maintain within 3 days.
Food accumulation on shelving holding spices above the wood prep table. Clean and maintain within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
