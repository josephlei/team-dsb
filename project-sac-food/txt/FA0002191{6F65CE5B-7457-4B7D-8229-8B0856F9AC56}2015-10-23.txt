#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Sliced tomato measured 47F on the prep counter. Tomatoes were relocated to the walk in. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Fan guards unclean in walk in cooler. Clean within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Pass placard missing from facility. Provided a new one at the time of inspection. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas.
Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating
that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
