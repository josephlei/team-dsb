# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Lack hot water at 3 comp sink. Correct immediately.
NOTE: No phf's exist on site.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot
water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Found garbage on the floor around all dumpsters. Correct today.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and other
conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 44c

MOP SINK AND JANITORIAL AREA

OBSERVATIONS: Found the mop inside liquid solution in the closet. Hang them in between uses or in the ringer.
immediately.

Correct

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: At least one janitorial sink equipped with hot and cold water and a drain shall be provided and conveniently
located for the cleaning of mops or similar wet floor cleaning tools and for the disposal of mop water and similar liquid waste. A room, area, or
cabinet separated from food preparation, food storage, or warewashing shall be provided for the storage of cleaning equipment and supplies.
After use, mops shall be placed in a position that allows them to air-dry without soiling walls, equipment, or supplies. (114279, 114281,
114282)
