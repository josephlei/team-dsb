Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential

#4

EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE

OBSERVATIONS: -Observed one employee drink with lid and straw storing on the front prep table. Employee drink was relocated to
the lowest section of nearby shelf during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and
utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to
prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for
employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from
contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: -Observed one insert of chicken, crab, and several inserts of sliced tomatoes storing inside the furthest north two
door reachin refrigerator at 45F. Ambient temperature of unit was observed at 45F. All above food was relocated to nearby two door
reachin refrigerator during inspection.
NOTE: Ensure that this unit is not used to store any potentially hazardous food until unit ambient temperature is 41F or below.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: -Hot water for the handwashing station below the front ventilation hood observed at 87F. Water temperature
began decreasing after 90 seconds of continuous running. Correct within 24 hours. Hot water at all hand washing stations must
dispense hot water at a minimum of 100F.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: -Old sauce spills and various food debris observed on sides and corners inside the front two door salad reachin
refrigerator. Clean and maintain within 48 hrs.
-Observed old grease and old food debris in on the sides of the stove and on the floor below the grill. Clean and maintain within 48
hours.
-Fanguard inside wine cooler is rusty. Clean and maintain. If fanguard has rusted beyond repair, replace within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: -Facility was unable to provide chlorine test strips for both the main dishwasher and the bar dishwasher. Correct
within 48 hours.
