# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: 1 of 2 pipes lack 2" air gap between floor sink and metal pipe to the right of the dish machine. Correct in 2 days.
2nd REPEAT VIOLATION
NOTE: Repeat of this violation may warrant a reinspection on all future inspections.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Missing hand washing signs inside restrooms in the lobby.

Provided.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food
facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet
facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).

# 47b

PLACARD POSTED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Placard relocated and partially blocked by a small reach in refrigerator for milk/eggs. Relocate to the wall near
the entry to the second dining room or on the glass doors to the facility. NOTE: Repeat of this violation may result in a
reinspection at $278 each day it is not in compliance.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Upon completion of a routine inspection or re-inspection, the Health Officer shall post at every food establishment the
appropriate placard, as determined by the Health Officer. The placard shall be posted so as to be clearly visible to the general public and to
patrons entering the food establishment. (Sacramento County Code 6.04.021)
