HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Found chicken breast on salad at 53F stored inside True cooler. Per employee, chicken salad was stored on top of
ice sheets at customer self-service area for 90 minutes and then was placed inside cooler. Corrected.
Minor Violation: Sliced cheeses (swiss/provolone) at 47F inside reach-in cooler. Per employee, cheese was stored out on the
counter top during lunch time. Do not store food items on the counter top without temperature control. Bring out on a needed bases
and store rest in refrigerator unit. Corrected.
Potentially hazardous foods may not be stored at room temperature unless time as a public health control (TPHC) is used. Store
foods at/below 41F or at/above 135F at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: - Lack of hot water at 2-comp warewash sink and handsink at the back prep area located inside warehouse area.
Food prep was not conducted at this location and cafeteria was closing at time of inspection. Another 2-comp warewash sink
located inside cafeteria with 127F of hot water. Maintenance arrived during inspection but was unable to fix this hot water issue.
Provide hot water of 120F to warewash sink and at least 100F to handwash sink at back prep area within 24 hours. There shall be no
food prep conducted in this area until required hot water is provided. Fax repair order to EMD attn: Elena within 3 days or
reinspection will apply to verify compliance.
- Hot water was between 109F-111F at not readily adjustable faucets inside women's restroom at admit building. Hot water from
these faucets shall be between 100F-108F. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)
