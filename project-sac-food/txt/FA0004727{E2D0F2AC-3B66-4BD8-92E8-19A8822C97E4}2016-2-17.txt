#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Handwashing sinks in Room 264 and at coffee station next to employee break room lack paper towels or soap.
Neither area was in use at the time of inspection. Provide within 24 hours or before opening areas. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Paper towel dispenser for handwashing sink closest to large ice machine is located across from sink, above clean utensils.
Relocate dispenser within 24 hours. Recommend placing on wall directly across from handwashing sink where nonfunctional
cold-top reach-in is located (see #44a).

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS:
Observed the following items out of proper holding temperature:
-cottage cheese (45F) located in cold-top insert on breakfast buffet line.
-milk (44F) located in 2-door Coke reach-in located in kitchen. Half & half in same unit measured 40F.
-diced ham (44F) and pooled eggs (44 F) stored in 4-door pass-thru reach-in located in pantry. Ambient temperature measured 40F.
Keep all potentially hazardous foods at/below 41F or at/above 135F. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to
the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
