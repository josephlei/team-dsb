#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser empty. Paper towels available on nearby counter. Manager does not have a key and is
unable to re-stock dispenser. Locate key or replace dispenser so that paper towels are kept stocked in dispenser at all times.
Correct 1 week.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: THIRD NOTICE: hot water at three compartment sink at 112F today. Repair so that hot water is readily available at
120F at all times. Correct 1 week.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Refrigerator is being used for staff personal food storage as well as some items for after school program.
Designate one area or shelf for staff items and label area "staff personal use only" so that food items are stored separate and away
from items used for food program. Correct 1 day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers
or other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)
