# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Lack of food managers certification available upon request. Provide within 6 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer bucket blocked handsink at point of sale. Relocated at the time of inspection. Corrected.
NOTE: Hand sink located in non prep area.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Hot water at dishmachine ran a maximum of 100F after 2 runs. Repari as necessary and discontinue use until
repaired. REPEAT VIOLATION.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 48

PLAN REVIEW CONSTRUCTION AND REMODELING

OBSERVATIONS: Several pieces of equipment have been brought into facility without EMD plan approval (convection unit, cold top
unit, coca cola merchandiser, El patron refrigerator and autochlor dish machine). Approval is required.
Per agreement with manager, equipment will get plans to plan review or remove equipment unapproved by plan review. Follow up
will be conducted in two weeks.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A person proposing to build or remodel a food facility shall submit plans for approval before starting any new
construction or remodeling of any facility for use as a retail food facility. (114380)
