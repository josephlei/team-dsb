HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Container with whipped cream 51F and container with cream cheese filling 55F stored above insert fill line at
customer self-service cooler. Per employee, these food items have been stored in this cooler since 10:30am. Potentially hazardous

at/above 135F. TPHC handout provided. Foods were relocated inside reach-in cooler. REPEAT VIOLATION

containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 5b

BARE HAND CONTACT AND GLOVE USE

OBSERVATIONS: Found used gloves hanging of ice shaver machine. Cease practice immediately. Single use gloves cannot be
reused. Employee discarded used gloves during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall minimize bare hand contact with nonprepackaged ready-to-eat food. Single-use gloves shall be
used for only one task and shall be discarded when damaged or soiled, or when interruptions in the food handling occur. Gloves shall be
changed as often as handwashing is required. Slash-resistant gloves may be used with ready-to-eat food if the gloves have a smooth,
durable, nonabsorbent surface, or are covered with a single-use glove. (113961, 113973 (b,c,e))

# 27

FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Raw shelled eggs stored above beverage inside walk-in cooler. Store raw product below ready-to-eat food/
beverage. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food
and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze
guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially
hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from
public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

FOOD PRESENTATION AND LABELING

OBSERVATIONS: The following food items missing required labeling and are stored at customer self-service area:
- packaged cookies and brownies missing ingredients
- packaged various flavors of frozen bananas, and yogurt cookies missing all information inside self-service freezer
- quarts of frozen sorbet tart missing ingredients inside self-service freezer
Provide labels on each package that include: common name of food, list of ingredients in descending order of predominance by

manufacturer, nutritional information as specified in 21 C.F.R. (114087, 114088, 114089, 114089.1(a, b), 114090, 114093.1)

COMPLIANCE WITH TRANS FAT BAN

OBSERVATIONS: - Per nutritional panel, cream cheese filling contains1 gram of trans fat per serving. (Serving size could not be
determined at time of inspection.) Foods that have 0.5 grams of trans fat per serving or higher shall not be served from facility.
Cease use of this product immediately.
- Dark chocolate flavored a'peels containers partially hydrogenated oil and nutritional panel is not available for review. (Per
employee, this food item is used only to dip waffle cones.) Also, nutritional panels are not available for all toppings. Obtain the

manufacturer's original sealed package is exempt from this requirement. (114377)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Facility is using chlorine as a sanitizer but has only quat ammonium test strips. Use quat ammonium sanitizer or
provide chlorine test strips within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used.
Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical
warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)

# 36

EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: - Open box with spoons and sample cups stored beneath front handwash sink plumbing. Store all equipment/food
in hygienic manner to prevent from possible contamination. Relocate immediately. REPEAT VIOLATION
- Two containers with spoons and scoops stored in stagnant (65F) water. Store in ice water 41F or below. Corrected by emptying all
water. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an
approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be
exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and
food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean
sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled,
displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely
fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3,
114185.4, 114185.5)

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Missing monitoring thermometer inside reach-in cooler. Provide within 5 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
(114157, 114159)

# 40

WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE

OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer bucket beneath front area stored without any sanitizer. Sanitizer bucket should be set up with 100 ppm
chlorine or 200 ppm quat ammonium. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water
with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact
surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).

OBSERVATIONS: Gaps/holes observed on ceiling and wall at dry storage room. Seal all holes/gaps with approved material to
