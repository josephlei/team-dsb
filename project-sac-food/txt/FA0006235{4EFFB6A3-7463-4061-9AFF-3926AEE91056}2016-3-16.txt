# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Dumpster lids open when not in use.
Lids stuck open between dumpster and enclosure.
Per employee, staff cannot move dumpster out of enclosure to close lids.
Waste hauler moved dumpster out of enclosure, emptied dumpster and did not close lids before pushing dumpster back into
enclosure.
Corrective action:
Contact property management and/or waste hauler to provide service that maintains lids in a position that can be closed after the
dumpster is emptied.
Facility staff is required to maintain the lids closed when it is not in use.
Notes:
1. More than 10 flies observed at dumpster.
2. Per operator, people regularly go through dumpster looking for food. Recommend locking dumpster to prevent access.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and
other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)
