HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Major
Observed fried chicken at 105F-127F in steamtable and rice and noodles at 120-129F in 1-door warmer. Maintain food hot at 135F or
above. Reheated during inspection. -Repeat Violation for steamtableMinor
Observed half & half at 46F inside display refrigerator. Maintain food cold at 41F or below. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to
the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 27

FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Uncovered containers of food inside refrigerators. Cover all food immediately. -Repeat Violation-

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food
and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards
that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or
uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access.
(113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Accumulation of grease and old food residue on floor beneath and on deep fryers and char-broiler, floor beneath

Clean and maintain. Correct within 7 days.
Accumulation of gease on ventilation hood system. Grease driplets observed.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: -Household food processor by Kenmore on shelf above back prep area and Mr. Coffee maker, Narita hot water
unit and Rival toaster oven located in front sales area. Discontinue use and remove within 30 days. Use only commercial
equipment. Employee claims all equipment in front sales area is personal use only. Please label "personal use".
-Deteriorated rubber gasket of left door of 2-door upright refrigerator. Repair within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods
shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and
scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3,
114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 36

EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: -Unsecured CO2 tanks in kitchen. Secure all pressurized tanks to rigid structure. Correct within 3 days.
-Rice scoop stored in standing 70F water near rice cooker. Must maintain utensils in cold (41F or below), hot (135F or above) or pH
adjusted water. If pH adjusted with vinegar, pH test strips is required. -Repeat Violation-

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an
approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be
exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and
food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean sanitized
surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled, displayed, and
dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely fastened to rigid
structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3, 114185.4, 114185.5)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Tallow/grease bin and garbage dumpster lids open. Keep lids closed when not in use. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be kept
in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at all
times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and other
conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Employee personal food and beverages stored on top of facility food inside 2-door prep refrigerator and water
bottle on shelf above prep table. Store all personal food and beverages below and away from food and food related equipment.
Also beverage container must have lid and straw.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers or
other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)

# 44e

VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION

OBSERVATIONS: Lack of self-closing mechanism for back door. Correct within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device and
have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food or
utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if required.
(114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: -Deteriorated (peeling paint) floor in warewashing area. Repair within 3 months. Must use commercial flooring
material. -Repeat Violation-1 broken floor tile beneath woks. Creating standing water. Repair within 30 days.
-Deteriorated grout between floor tiles near 2-door upright refrigerator. Repair within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving
shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture
of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1,
114271, 114272)
