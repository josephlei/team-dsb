VIOLATIONS AND CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
.7.

MAJOR VIOLATION:
Fried chicken at 98-125F. BBQ pork at 122F. Sweet and sour pork at 125F. All items were reheated to 165F or greater. Water level in steam table was too low.
Ensure the water touches the bottom of the pan. Corrected during inspection. There are not enough doors on the hot case to keep the case fully closed.
Recommend getting more doors to ensure hot food stays at/above 135F.
Note: Kitchen is located on the other side from where the food is kept hot. The employees are cooking in large batches and placing on rolling carts until all the
food is complete before rolling over to be kept inside hot case. Ensure that food is placed in steam table immediately after cooking. It can not sit on the carts.
Recommend getting a hot holding case/cabinet to keep food hot during the preparation time.
27.

Raw shelled eggs stored above cooked food and vegetables. Store eggs below and away from ready to eat food. Correct today.
29.

Can of air freshner stored with food inside deli kitchen. Corrected by removing can.
30c

Containers of dixie flour and flour are not labeled in dry storage area before kitchen entrance. Label containers within 3 days.
35.

-Plastic container used as a scoop in dixie flour. Provide a scoop with a handle and store in a manner to prevent handle from touching food. Correct within 3
days.
-Cardboard covered in aluminum foil lining shelving inside walk in refrigerator in deli kitchen. Remove cardboard lining to allow air to circulate through shelving.
Correct within 2 days (REPEAT VIOLATION).
36.

Handles of scoops in contact with food stored in bulk containers in deli kitchen. Store scoop handles so that their handles do not touch the food. Correct within
2 days.
41.

Hose attached to faucet of preparation sink lacks an air gap. Remove hose or provide a minimum of one inch from end of hose and top rim of sink.
Note: Prep sink is indirectly connect to the nearby floor sink.
