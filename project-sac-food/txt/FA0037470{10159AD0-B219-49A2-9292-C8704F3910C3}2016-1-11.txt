#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Container with cheesecake filling 63F and whipped cream 44F stored above insert fill line at customer self-service
cooler. Per employee, these items have been placed inside insert at 10:45am. Maintain at/below 41F at all times. Manager relocated
food inside reach-in cooler. Highly recommend providing different cooling element for storage of these food items to ensure dairy is
stored at proper holding temperature at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Back handwash station next to 3-comp sink was partially blocked by push table. Manager was educated.
Corrected. REPEAT VIOLATION Non-compliance may result in a major violation.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

OBSERVATIONS: - Unclean cutting board stored on table next to warewash sink. Clean properly and maintain. If it cannot be
cleaned then replace. Correct today.
- Soiled cloth towel was found next to crepe maker. Per manager, this cloth towel is used to wipe heated crepe surface and it's
replaced once a week. Replace cloth towel at least daily or more often. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each
use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and

Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)

# 32a

FOOD PRESENTATION AND LABELING

OBSERVATIONS: Various cookies from Cookie Connection missing ingredients at customer self-service area. Provide labels on
each package that include: common name of food, list of ingredients in descending order of predominance by weight, quantity, and
name and place of business of the manufacturer, packer, or distributer. Correct within 7 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food offered for human consumption shall be honestly presented in a way that does not mislead or misinform the
consumer. Food packaged in a food facility shall bear a label that complies with the labeling requirements prescribed by the Sherman Food,
Drug, and Cosmetic Law which include: common name of the food, ingredients list, quantity of contents, name and address of
manufacturer, nutritional information as specified in 21 C.F.R. (114087, 114088, 114089, 114089.1(a, b), 114090, 114093.1)

# 32b

COMPLIANCE WITH TRANS FAT BAN

OBSERVATIONS: - Cheese cake filling contains 1 gram of trans fat per serving. Per manager, this is new topping that they have
started offering. Foods that have 0.5 grams of trans fat per serving or higher shall not be served from facility. Discontinue use
immediately.
- Facility missing trans fat information for the following food items that contain partially hydrogenated oils: dark raspberry mini cup,
cappuccino chips, and chopped whoppers. Panels must be available for verification that all food served does not contain artificial
trans fat. Obtain within 14 days. REPEAT VIOLATION
Note: Trans fat ban handout located inside finder along with food handler cards.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No food containing artificial trans fat may be stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of any food
within a food facility. Every food facility shall maintain on the premises, the label for any food that contains fat, oil, or shortening, for as long
as this food is stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of food within the food facility. Food sold or served in a
manufacturer's original sealed package is exempt from this requirement. (114377)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Faucet at 3-comp sink unable to reach all three compartments. Install proper faucet that would be able to reach all
three compartment sinks. Correct within 5 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used.
Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical
warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Missing monitoring thermometer inside reach-in cooler. Provide within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
(114157, 114159)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Dipper well and yogurt machine (clear pipe) directly connected into sewage drain located beneath front handwash
sink. Also, shaved ice drainage tube found inside front handwash sink. Provide proper indirect drainage for dipper well, yogurt
machine, and ice shaver within 5 days. Provide plans for drainage to EMD Plan Review for approval. (Also, see violation #48)
REPEAT VIOLATION
Note: Existing floor sink located about 28 feet away from dipper well.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Missing outlet cover behind yogurt machine that is located in front area behind gelato refrigerator. Install within
14 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)

# 48

PLAN REVIEW CONSTRUCTION AND REMODELING

OBSERVATIONS: During routine inspection performed on 04-02-15 it was noted that facility installed new equipment without EMD
Plan Review approval (including, but not limited to, additional freezers, gelato display freezer, and ice shaver). Front yogurt machine
also seems to be newly installed since new electrical connection was observed behind machine. Operator opted not to have a
facility at time of permit application. Operator must submit changes to EMD Plan Review (916) 874-6010 within 5 days.
Non-compliance will result in a reinspection(s) $268 each. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: A person proposing to build or remodel a food facility shall submit plans for approval before starting any new
construction or remodeling of any facility for use as a retail food facility. (114380)
