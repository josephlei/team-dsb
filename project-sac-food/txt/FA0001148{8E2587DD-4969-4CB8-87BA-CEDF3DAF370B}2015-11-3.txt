# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed one dead cockroach behind refrigeration unit inside small prep area in the back room. Overall facility
seems to be clean. Remove cockroach and clean behind refrigerators. Correct within one day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Observed two large buckets of frozen meats improperly thawing at room temperature beneath steam table. Use
approved thawing methods. Provided proper thawing education and handout. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Fan guards inside refrigeration units are accumulated with black residue. Clean and sanitize within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 47c

INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY

OBSERVATIONS: Most recent routine inspection report not available. Keep inside facility at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be
maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the
most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
