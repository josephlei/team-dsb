#8

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

OBSERVATIONS: Fried zucchini (122F) and grilled pineapple (94F) are stored under Time as a Public Health Control (TPHC) without
required time documentation. Corrected; food items were voluntarily discarded during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be
marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within
the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was
used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Hot water at the 3-compartment sink warewash sink measured 115F. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: A knife with deteriorated plastic handle is observed in the wall-mounted knife storage box. Replace deteriorated
knife within 3 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare
foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching
and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2,
114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Floor cove base tiles behind kitchen hand sink, behind warewash sink, and near walk-in refrigerator door are
damaged. Replace within 45 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION-2ND NOTICE)
Floor tiles missing grout below kitchen hand sink. Re-grout within 45 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)
2"-3" opening is observed in ceiling above chicken tender breading station. Seal opening within 14 days. (REPEAT VIOLATION)

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
