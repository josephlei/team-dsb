# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Numerous fruit flies in ware washing area. Remove immediately and maintain.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Fresh mozzarella improperly thawing stacked on top of pepperoni in cold top insert. Correct immediately. NOTE:
cheese measured 32F.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: The following areas observed with old food debris:
*floor throughout facility behind all equipment and shelving
*in between floor tiles throughout facility
*light switches
*handles to refrigeration units
*handles to plumbing faucets
*walls in ware washing area
*floor sinks and drains in ware washing area
*walls in walk in cooler
*vinyl cove base, currently in good condition, in dough room
Correct within 72 hours and maintain. NOTE: highly recommend commercial cleaning service.
Paint buckets and painting equipment creating clutter near soda syrup boxes. Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Cardboard used as a floor liner near hand sink at pizza make line. Correct today.
Plastic bag, filling with brown water and splattered with food debris, draped over soda syrup boxes and pump. Correct today.
Containers without handles used as scoops in bulk food containers in dough room. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare
foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching
and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2,
114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 40

WIPING CLOTHS STORAGE AND USE

OBSERVATIONS: 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION
Sanitizer buckets at pizza make line measured 0 ppm sanitizer residual. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Wiping cloths used to clean food and nonfood-contact surfaces shall be used only once unless kept in clean water
with a sanitizer solution of 100 ppm available chlorine or 200 ppm quaternary ammonium. Sponges shall not be used on food-contact
surfaces. Except for fabric implements, linen shall not be used in contact with food. (114099.6, 114135, 114185.1, 114185.3 (d-e)).

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Missing grout and/or low grout throughout facility allowing old food and water accumulation. Deep clean floors
within 72 hours and re-grout within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Expired health permit posted on wall. Correct within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas.
Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating
that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
