HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Unenclosed paper towel dispenser at handwashing sink. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: No monitoring thermometer in salad/sandwich display cooler. Provide thermometer and situate it at the warmest
part of the unit and where it is easily readable. Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
