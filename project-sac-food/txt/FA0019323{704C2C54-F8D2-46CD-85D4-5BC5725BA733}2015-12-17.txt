Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700.

#8

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: Lack logs on the following potentially hazardous food items on the line: waffle batter, shredded cheese, boiled
eggs, deli meats, sliced cheeses, cut fruit and oatmeal. Provide logs or hold below 41F and or above 135F.
Correct immediately

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be marked
to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within the
four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was used,
shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Lack 100-108F water supply on sensor hand sink in the women's restroom(near the pool).
NOTE: Todays reading at 72F.

Correct immediately

REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 27

FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Lack sneeze guard protection over the following items: different types of bread, salsa, spinach, oatmeal
condiments(6), shredded cheese and apples. Correct all in 1 day.
REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food
and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze guards
that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially hazardous food or
uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from public access.
(113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Last inspection report unavailable upon request. Recommend placing a copy on the wall next to the food cards
and the manager certificate since management does not arrive until after 8am. Correct immediately
REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas. Food
facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating that toilet
facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
