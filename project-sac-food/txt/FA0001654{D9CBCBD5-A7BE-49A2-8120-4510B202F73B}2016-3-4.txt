#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Beef taquitos at 119F, chicken taquito at 124F at hot dog grill. Maintain at 135F or above. Discarded voluntarily.
<REPEAT VIOLATION>

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Food safety certificate expired. Provide one food safety certified employee within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#8

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

OBSERVATIONS: The timer for the food items in the hot case (pizza, cheese sticks, chicken wings, egg rolls) was not set.
Temperature of all of them were above 135F.
Track time and discard when time expires. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be
marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within
the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was
used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Floor under soda syrup rack unclean. Clean within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: 0 ppm sanitizer at the dispenser at 3 compartment sink. No dishwashing observed during inspection.
Corrected by replacing the sanitizer.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used.
Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical
warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)
