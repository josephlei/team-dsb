VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: German cockroaches observed in the following locations:
One live nymph on floor below bate box.
One dying juvenile on door jam between dough prep and dry storage.
One live nymph and one live juvenile below electrical box in storage room.
One live juvenile inside ice machine in storage room.
One dead roach in door panel of ice machine with feces from roaches inside machine in working parts.
Two dead below ice machine and two dead below drain board at warewash sink.
One dead juvenile by water heater at coving on right side.
Facility was treated last night for a second time by pest control. Areas where live roaches were observed need to be re-treated. Ice
machine must be cleaned in all areas affected by roaches but chemicals cannot be used in ice bin or any area that can contaminate
ice or its production.
Call for reinspection when infestation no longer exists.
