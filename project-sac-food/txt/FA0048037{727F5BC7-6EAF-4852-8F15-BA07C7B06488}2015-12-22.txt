#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: At time of arrival handwash sink was completely blocked by trays. Employee removed trays in order for inspector
to wash hands. Keep handwash sink clear and easily accessible at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE

OBSERVATIONS: Employee bottled beverage stored on metro shelf above straws. All employee beverages must be stored in a cup
with lid and straw, and located below food and food related items. Bottle was relocated during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and
utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to
prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for
employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from
contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))

# 32b

COMPLIANCE WITH TRANS FAT BAN

OBSERVATIONS: - Ice cream cake icing contains shortening and waffle dry mixture contains partially hydrogenated oil as an
ingredient and facility was unable to provide trans fat nutritional panel for review.
- Missing nutritional panels for cake/ice cream decorations.
Per owner, she contacted manufacture but they were unable to provide her with any documentation. Nutritional panels shall be
available to verify that all non-prepackaged food items are trans-fat free. Foods that have 0.5 grams of trans fat per serving or
higher shall not be served from facility. All panels shall be available within 30 days. Owner will be contacting manufactures again.
REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: No food containing artificial trans fat may be stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of any food
within a food facility. Every food facility shall maintain on the premises, the label for any food that contains fat, oil, or shortening, for as long
as this food is stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of food within the food facility. Food sold or served in a
manufacturer's original sealed package is exempt from this requirement. (114377)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Anti-siphon device missing between Y connector and a hose at mop sink. Install backflow prevention device
within 5 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
