#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: The following food items were located in the prep cooler against the wall are in the major violation range.
Mixed cheese at 54F, steak at 53F, roasted garlic at 52F, sausage at 54F, feta cheese at 53F.
Maintain cold food at 41F or below. Unit has an internal temperature of 57F. Have unit service with in 24 hours. Do not store
potentially hazardous food in this unit until it has been serviced.
MINOR VIOLATION
Cut tomatoes in the cold top, diced chicken, and mozzarella cheese at 50F. Maintain cold food at 41F or below. Items were removed
and placed into the walk in cooler.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
