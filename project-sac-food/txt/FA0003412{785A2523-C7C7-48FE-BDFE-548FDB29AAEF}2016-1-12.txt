#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Fried fish in shallow pan at end of Taqueria steam table (closest to beverages on ice) was at 112-132F. Per
operator the items in shallow pans are for sampling. When a customer asks for a sample they fry it before serving to customers. All
potentially hazardous foods must be kept cold at/below 41F or hot at/above 135F. Facility can use Time as A Public Health Control
(TPHC) where the food item must be marked with the discard time which is 4 hours after the food was removed from temperature
control. This can be done with the log provided or time stamp sticker. The food must be discarded at the end of the 4 hours. Food
cannot be placed backed into refrigeration or cooked for later use.
Roasted half chicken on steam table under heat lamps on Cocina side was at 120-124F. It was placed out there at 9am and operator
stated they could not reheat it. Operator voluntarily discarded chicken at time of inspection.

OBSERVATIONS: Grease accumulation on table to the right of fryer. Clean and maintain within 3 days.

# 36

EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Condiment type cup used as a scoop inside the salt container on rack above prep table in back kitchen. Provide a
scoop with a handle and store in a manner to prevent handle from touching the food. Correct within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an
approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be
exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and
food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean
sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled,
displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely
fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3,
114185.4, 114185.5)
