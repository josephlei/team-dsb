HANDWASHING FACILITIES

MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: REPEAT VIOLATION: MAJOR- Paper towel dispenser in the restroom does not dispense towels. Ensure paper
towel dispenser is always working correctly.
Currently using a terry hand towel hung up by door to back room. Remove cloth towel and use single use (paper) towels in an
enclosed dispenser. If the towel dispenser that you have does not work properly, repair or replace with working dispenser. Correct
within 3 days.
CORRECTED BY PLACING PAPER TOWELS IN THE RESTROOM.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: REPEAT VIOLATION: Restroom hand sink lacks hot water. Provide hot water within 2 days. Water faucet must
work appropriately, with no drips or leaks.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Employee personal items, including a microwave and single burner, lack labels indicating that these are for
employee use. Either remove and provide commercial grade equipment, or label. Keep all employee items in a designated area and
separate from retail food and supplies. Correct within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers or
other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)
