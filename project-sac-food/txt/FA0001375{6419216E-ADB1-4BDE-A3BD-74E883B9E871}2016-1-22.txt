#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: 2% milk (44F) and soymilk (44F) in the reach-in below the cappuccino machine were out of proper holding
temperature. Maintain potentially hazardous foods (PHF) at proper holding temperature at all times. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Dead cockroaches (six adult, one nymph) were observed on the traps at four different locations within the facility.
Contact pest control service regarding potential visit to determine if there is an active infestation present within the facility.
According to pest control report from service provided 3 days ago, no presence of roaches (dead or alive were observed). A
re-inspection will be performed within 14 days to determine if there is an active infestation present.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 43

RESTROOMS MAINTAINED, SUPPLIED, AND ACCESSIBLE

OBSERVATIONS: One of the two unisex community shared restrooms is lacking a toilet paper dispenser. Service call has been
made to the plaza management. Numerous postings regarding "out of order" status had been provided but keeps getting removed.
Provide posting and check regularly to direct customers to use functional restroom. Correct immediately. Provide dispenser within
3 days. Contact plaza management to lock the restroom. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Toilet facilities shall be maintained and kept clean. Toilet rooms shall be separated by a well-fitting self-closing door.
Toilet tissue shall be provided in a permanently installed dispenser at each toilet. The number of toilet facilities shall be in accordance with
local building and plumbing ordinances. Toilet facilities shall be provided for patrons: in establishments with more than 20,000 sq. ft.;
establishments offering on-site consumption of food. (114250, 114250.1, 114276)
