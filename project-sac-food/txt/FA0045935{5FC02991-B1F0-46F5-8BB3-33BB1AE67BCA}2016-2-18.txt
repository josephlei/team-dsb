Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Found 1 dying cockroach on glue pad on floor under cold top cooler in salad prep area of Jacks Urban Eats. No
other cockroach activity observed during inspection. Pest control stated there was activity seen this morning but cockroaches
were dying due to contact with application used. Those cockroaches were killed at that time. Continue working with pest control to
eliminate all cockroach activity. Reinspection will be conducted within 14 days to ensure compliance.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Floor under hood in Jacks Urban Eats front service area noted with grease. Bottom of deep fryer, back of reach-in
refrigerator under front hood in Jacks also noted with grease buildup. Old food debris noted inside drawer cooler under front hood
of Jacks. Old food debris in tortilla chip warmer and drawer cooler in Dos Coyotes front service area. Minor debris around post of
tables along walls in shared kitchen. Grease on wall near electrical outlets under front counter of Jacks Urban Eats. Floor under
ice machine and under warewash sink noted with debris and/or grease in shared kitchen. Clean within 24 hours and maintain.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Salad prep cooler was relocated downstairs for cleaning. Provide proof of repair for condensation leak within 6
days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare
foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching
and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2,
114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed deteriorated grout between all floor tiles in Dos Coyotes, Jacks and shared kitchen. Broken coving tiles
found in Dos Coyotes, shared kitchen and Jacks. Repair or replace. Repeat violation. Management to provide proof of progress
(work order, invoice, contract, etc.) within 6 days to prevent additional reinspection for flooring. SSP management stated contractor
will be replacing broken and missing tiles throughout all three areas within the next couple days and re-grouting shortly after.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
