#4

EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE

OBSERVATIONS: Observed staff eating his lunch from kitchen preparation table. Relocate immediately to dining area or
designated areas away from kitchen.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and
utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to prevent
contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for employees to
eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from contamination.
(113977, 114256 (a))

# 30a

FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Several boxes of food stored on the walk in freezer floor. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed
permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged food
may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging.
Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. (114047, 114049, 114053, 114055, 114069 (b))

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Areas observed with old food/grease/residue like accumulation:
-bottom side to top doors on cold top cooler
-several bulk food containers under dry storage rack across walk in cooler
-light switch panel in dry storage closet
REPEAT VIOLATION:
-door handles to 4 door reach in cooler 2ND
Correct within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Test strips for quaternary ammonium sanitizer is not available. Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used. Testing
materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical warewashing.
(113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Vegetables stored directly in domestic non food grade plastic bags. Relocate into clear food grade bags within
24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Unlabeled personal rice cooker located on boxes of food by walk in cooler. Unit labeled, however, remove away
from consumer goods today.
Personal food/belong stored throughout kitchen. Relocate to a desginated area. Correct today.
REPEAT VIOLATION:
Unlabeled personal food processor located on preparation table. Corrected. 4TH.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers or
