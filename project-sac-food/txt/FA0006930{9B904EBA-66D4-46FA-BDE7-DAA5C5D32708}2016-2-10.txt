# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS:
One live, slow and randomly moving nymph was on the floor behind the water heater room door. Corrected by catching it with a
paper towel.
One dying cockroach that was flipped over on its back, was on the floor in front of the water heater. Corrected.
Approximately 30 dead nymphs altogether were on the floor corner under the prep sink and back corners and floor sides of the
water heater. About 6 dead ones were in the floor sink in the water heater room. Remove all dead bodies and thoroughly clean all
affected areas.
Note: Pest control service treated facility yesterday.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 32a

FOOD PRESENTATION AND LABELING

OBSERVATIONS: Containers of bilo bilo for customer self-service on the front counter lack labels. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION.
Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food offered for human consumption shall be honestly presented in a way that does not mislead or misinform the
consumer. Food packaged in a food facility shall bear a label that complies with the labeling requirements prescribed by the Sherman Food,
Drug, and Cosmetic Law which include: common name of the food, ingredients list, quantity of contents, name and address of manufacturer,
nutritional information as specified in 21 C.F.R. (114087, 114088, 114089, 114089.1(a, b), 114090, 114093.1)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS:
Floor behind and under all cooking equipment and Blodgett oven has grease and debris buildup. Correct within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS:
The following areas need repair:
-gap between cove base tile and floor in the holding cabinet area to the right of the steam table
-large gap on cove base floor tile and floor junction behind the water heater
Correct within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving
shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture
of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1,
114271, 114272)
