# 13

FOOD IN GOOD CONDITION, SAFE, AND UNADULTERATED

OBSERVATIONS:
MAJOR VIOLATION
-Observed 2 roses in direct contact with ice in one ice machine and mold-like slime in direct contact with ice inside second ice
machine. Ice was voluntarily discarded. Wash, rinse and sanitize interior of both ice machines before refilling.
-Also observed 6-pack container of beer stored inside ice machine in direct contact with ice. Ice was discarded.
MINOR VIOLATION
-Observed approximately 8 drain flies inside bottle of liquor. Item was voluntarily discarded.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not commit any act that may cause the contamination or adulteration of food or food contact
surfaces. Food shall be produced, prepared, stored, transported, and served, so as to be free from adulteration and spoilage. Food is
considered adulterated if it bears or contains any poisonous or deleterious substance that may render it impure or injurious to health.
Containers previously used to store poisonous or toxic materials shall not be used to store food, utensils, or single-use articles. Utensils
used for tasting shall be discarded or washed, rinsed, and sanitized between tastings. Ice used as a medium for cooling food and
beverages shall not be offered for consumption. Food shall be inspected upon receipt and prior to any use, storage, or resale. (113967,
113976, 113980, 113988, 113990, 114035, 114167, 114254.3)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser in front women's restroom is nonfunctional. It appears that the batteries are dead. Correct
immediately.
1 of 2 soap dispensers in back women's restroom is empty. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 14a

FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED

OBSERVATIONS: 2 of 3 bar soda guns have grime build up. 1 of 3 soda guns have small fly (drain fly?) egg sacks attached to
interior nozzle. Wash, rinse and sanitize immediately. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Interior deflector plates of ice machines have black mold-like slime. Also observed the same material on exterior surface of ice
machine where water from ice maker is leaking over surface and going into ice storage of machine. Correct immediately.
<<REPEAT VIOLATION>>

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each
use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and
potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred.
Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Front bar lacks hot water at the handwashing sink. Correct within 7 days.

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed approximately 10 drain flies throughout front bar. Eliminate insects immediately.
Observed 1 dying German cockroach on side of ice bin located in front service bar. No other cockroach activity was observed.
Remove all standing water and seal all cracks, crevices and gaps inside area to prevent vermin harborage. Provide professional
pest control to treat facility. Send pest report to inspector via fax or email (attention S. LePage, 916-875-8513 or
lepages@saccounty.net).

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Standing water observed throughout front bar and side bar. Remove standing water today.
Observed trash and debris not cleaned up since facility was last open the previous weekend. Facility must be left in a clean
condition after closing each night to prevent attracting vermin. Correct immediately.
Observed mold like accumulation on floor inside kegerator reach-in. Clean and sanitize within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 36

EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: CO2 tank is unsecured to a rigid structure. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an
approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be
exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and
food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean
sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled,
displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely
fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3,
114185.4, 114185.5)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Dumpster next to back service entrance is left open. Keep closed when not in use. Correct today.

Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Floors in front bar are heavily deteriorated with exposed concrete aggregate throughout, making it impossible to
keep clean and dry. Repair floor with approved materials within 30 days. Handout provided.
Deteriorated linoleum flooring observed throughout back kitchen area. Many areas of linoleum are coming apart at the seams and
around the cove base. Remove linoleum and replace with approved flooring within 30 days. Handout provided.
Light above soda syrup storage is out of place, creating a large gap in the ceiling. Replace light within 7 days.
Observed 33 missing floor tiles inside stall of back women's restroom. Repair within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
