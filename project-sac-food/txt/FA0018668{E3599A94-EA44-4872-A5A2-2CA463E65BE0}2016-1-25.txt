# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Maximum hot water at ware wash sink was 112F and no hot water available in employee restrooms. Provide hot
water of at least 120F at the ware wash sink and 100-108F at the hand wash stations. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Dust build up observed on fan guards of True branded reach in refrigerator. Clean and maintain within 48 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Restrooms lack hand wash stickers. Provided at time of inspection. Post today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas.
Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating
that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).

# 49

PERMIT REQUIREMENTS

OBSERVATIONS: Current health permit is not posted. Post current health permit in conspicuous location.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall not be open for business without a valid permit. Permit shall be posted in a conspicuous place
within the food facility. (114067(b, c), 114381, 114387)
