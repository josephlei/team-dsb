HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser and soap dispenser at the hand wash station (ware wash sink "Wash" area is used as hand

times. Corrected at time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Found dead/dying Oriental cockroaches in the following areas:
-two loose adults on floor below Manitowoc reach in refrigerator
-one dead on glue trap on floor below Manitowoc reach in refrigerator
-one adult on glue trap below Manitowoc reach in refrigerator located directly in front of desk
-one dying adult on floor below ware wash sink
-5 dead adults on glue trap below convection oven
Clean and maintain areas. Per person in charge, school is serviced by internal pest control personnel. Continue aggressive pest
control to ensure facility is cockroach free.
No active live cockroaches found during today's inspection. A re inspection may be conducted within 14 days to verify that facility is
cockroach free. A re inspection fee of $268.00 may apply.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)
