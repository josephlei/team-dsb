# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS:
Observed the following German cockroach activity:
-2 dead nymphs on glue trap located on floor behind 1-door reach-in cooler under Ansul system
-1 live nymph on glue trap located on counter next to same 1-door reach-in cooler
-1 dead nymph in corner of shelving under counter next to same 1-door reach-in cooler
-1 dying nymph under open cabinet next to floor sink for drive-thru soda machine, across from 1-door reach-in cooler. Cockroach
was killed by operator at the time of inspection.
Observed glue traps lining the floor behind cold-top reach-in that is next to walk-in cooler where activity was observed during
previous inspection. No activity was observed here today.
According to operator, wooden shelves and cabinets next to 1-door reach-in where activity is centered is scheduled for demolition
today. Shelves are being replaced with metal wire storage racks.
Facility is being continually treated by Advanced Integrated Pest Management and has been treated on 2/5/16 and 2/9/16. According
to operator, facility will be treated again on either 2/12/16 or 2/16/16.
No other vermin activity was observed at the time of inspection. A reinspection will be conducted within 7-10 days to ensure an
active infestation does not exist.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Household Kitchen Aid mixer observed on counter top under Ansul fire suppression system. Remove from facility
within 3 months and replace with ANSI/NSF approved commercial grade equipment only.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare
foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching
and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2,
114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed broken tile in women's restroom. Repair within 21 days.
Observed unapproved wall surface above mop sink. Repair wall so that it is smooth, durable and nonabsorbent. Correct within 21
days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
