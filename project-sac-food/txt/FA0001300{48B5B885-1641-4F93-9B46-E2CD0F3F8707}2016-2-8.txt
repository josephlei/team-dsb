#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed chicken at 117F on top of covered steam table. Chicken tenders at 119F in pan on top of flat grill.
Tomatoes at 61F on ice, grilled peppers at 58F. Maintain cold food at 41F or below, hot food at 135F or higher.
Garlic butter at 64F on the table. Maintain at 41F or below.
Cold food were put into cooler. Hot food were reheated to 165F.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: No food safety certificate. Correct in 60 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

# 5a

HANDWASHING PROCEDURES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed employee change gloves and was about to put on new gloves with out washing hands. Corrected
during inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees are required to wash their hands in the following situations: before engaging in food preparation, after
touching bare skin, after using the toilet room, after coughing or sneezing, after eating, drinking, or using tobacco, after handling soiled
equipment or utensils, as often as necessary during food preparation to prevent cross-contamination, when switching from working with raw
to ready to eat foods, before donning gloves, before dispensing or serving food, after engaging in other activities that may contaminate
hands. (113952, 113953.3, 113953.4)

#9

COOLING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Observed cut chicken at 64F in covered container in the walk in cooler. Uncover food when cooling. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be rapidly cooled from 135F to 70F within 2 hours, and then from 70F to 41F within
4 hours. Cooling shall be by one or more of the following methods: in shallow containers, separating food into smaller portions, adding ice
as an ingredient, using an ice bath, stirring frequently, using rapid cooling equipment, or using containers that facilitate heat transfer.
(114002, 114002.1)

# 29

IDENTIFICATION AND STORAGE OF TOXIC SUBSTANCES

OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer buckets stored on top of counter by prep surface next to hand wash sink in the cookline. Store
chemicals away from food and food related equipment. Correct.
