Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Lack pump style hand soap dispenser at north bar. Provide pump style dispenser within 3 days. NOTE: hand soap
currently available in squeeze bottle.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed ~10 dried rodent droppings on floor of water heater room. Remove and sanitize immediately. NOTE:
pest control service is provided monthly. Last pest control service provided on 1/13/16.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Mold like debris in floor sink located below ice machine near door. Correct within 3 days and maintain.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Unsecured pressurized cylinders in cupboard at north bar. Secure to rigid structure within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare
foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching
and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2,
114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Drain lines for ice machine #2 draining onto floor. Correct today and maintain.
Plumbing from bar well #1 observed draining onto floor near floor sink at east bar. Correct today and maintain.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)

# 44e

VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed gaps greater than 1/4" surrounding ducts, plumbing, and attic space in water heater room. Correct
within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage
of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device
and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food
or utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if
required. (114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)

# 47a

SIGNS POSTED AS REQUIRED

OBSERVATIONS: Most recent health permit is not posted. Correct within 7 days. Contact EMD at 916-875-8440 to request an
electronic copy.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing signs directing employees to wash hands after using the restroom shall be posted in each toilet room
used by food employees. No smoking signs shall be posted in food preparation, food storage, warewashing, and utensil storage areas.
Food facilities constructed without a public toilet prior January 1, 2004, shall post a sign in a prominant area within the food facility, stating
that toilet facilities are not provided. (113953.5, 113978, 114276 (f)).
