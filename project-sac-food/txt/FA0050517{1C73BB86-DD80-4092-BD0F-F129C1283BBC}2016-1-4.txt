DEMONSTRATION OF KNOWLEDGE

OBSERVATIONS: Employees report not having sanitizer available for washing utensils as it is on order. Facility has bleach
available for other cleaning. Facility has limited multi use utensils (tongs, trays, domes). Multi use utensils that are used with
