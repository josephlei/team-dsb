# 1a

DEMONSTRATION OF KNOWLEDGE

OBSERVATIONS: All hand sink water temperatures were cold at time of inspection. Hot water was not reached until after 3-5
minutes. Staff utilizing cold water for hand washing. Ensure hand sink water temperatures are at 100F prior to utilizing. Provided
education and corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food employees shall have adequate knowledge of food safety as it relates to their assigned duties. (113947)

# 11

REHEATING PROCEDURES FOR HOT HOLDING

OBSERVATIONS: Meatballs in tomato sauce measured at 125F in steam table. Unit is ambient at 156F. Item was not reheated to
135F before staging in steam table. Item reheated to proper temperatures at time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods that are cooked, cooled, and subsequently reheated for hot holding or service, shall be
heated to a temperature of 165F for 15 seconds. Ready-to-eat food taken from a commercailly processed hermetically sealed container, or
from an intact package from a food processing plant shall be heated to a temperature of 135F for hot holding. (114016)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Frozen bags of Alfredo sauce left thawing unattended in a container in prepartion sink without proper
implementations. Ensure items are thawed in a cooler or thaw in a microwave and utilize immediately. Correct now.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)

Correct within 3 days.

debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 38

VENTILATION AND LIGHTING

OBSERVATIONS: 1 non functional light bulb in middle hood ventilation. Correct within 7 days. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION.
Several damaged filters observed in hood ventilations systems. Correct within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Exhaust hoods shall be provided to remove toxic gases, heat, grease, vapors and smoke and be approved by health
and building department. Canopy-type hoods shall extend six inches beyond all cooking equipment. All areas shall have sufficient ventilation
to facilitate proper food storage. Toilet rooms shall be vented to the outside air by a screened openable window, air shaft, or light-switch
activated exhaust fan. Adequate lighting shall be provided in all areas to facilitate cleaning and inspection. Light fixtures in areas where open
food is stored, served, prepared, and where utensils are washed shall be of shatterproof construction or protected with light shields. (114149,
114149.1, 114149.2, 114149.3, 114252, 114252.1)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Old food and trash debris observed in outdoor garbage enclosure. Corrected. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be kept
in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at all
times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and other
conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Door frame leading from kitchen to cook's line is no longer smooth and easily cleanable. Repair within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving
shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture
of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1,
114271, 114272)
