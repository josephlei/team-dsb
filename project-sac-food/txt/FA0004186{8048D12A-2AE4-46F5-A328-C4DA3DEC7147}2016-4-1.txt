#8

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

OBSERVATIONS: Major Violation
Self service milk in carafes lacked a tracking method. Per operator, timer listed as "sanitizer time" is being used for both the
sanitizer bucket and milk carafes. Discontinue practice and provide the carafes with either a separate timer listed as "milk carafe"
time or provide a log showing discard time (4 hours). Milk was voluntarily discarded by operator during time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be
marked to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within
the four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was
used, shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Dumpster lids were opened during inspection. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and
other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 47c

INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY

OBSERVATIONS: Facility was unable to provide the most current inspection report.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be
maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the
most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
