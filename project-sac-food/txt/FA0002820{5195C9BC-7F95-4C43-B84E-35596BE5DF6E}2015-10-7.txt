Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700.

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Approximately 6 dead Turkestan cockroaches observed in a glueboard monitoring station on floor below cash
registers. Observed 1 live nymph on same glueboard. Provide pest control services within 24 hours. Fax / email copy of pest
control report to inspector (916-875-8513 or lepages@saccounty.net). Facility received monthly pest control from Clark Pest
Control.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Observed grease and food debris accumulation under cooking equipment. Clean to prevent attracting vermin
within 7 days.
NOTE: Current gas lines prevent equipment from being moved easily so that cleaning is more difficult. Make repairs as discussed
so equipment can be moved frequently for appropriate cleaning.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Observed unapproved expanding foam that my allow harborage points for vermin. Foam is also not cleanable.
Replace foam with a caulk that is smooth, durable and easily cleanable; or make foam smooth and flush with surrounding wood
and paint/seal so that it is smooth, durable and easily cleanable. Correct within 30 days.
Observed large gap at the top of FRP wall between the cashier area and the cooking equipment. Carefully inspect each and every
part of facility and seal any gaps, cracks and crevices with approved materials to prevent vermin entry and harborage. Correct
within 60 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods
shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and
scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3,
114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 44e

VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed tears in 2 window screens. Repair / replace before opening for business.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be constructed, equipped, and maintained as to prevent the entrance and harborage
of animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to, rodents and insects. Doors opening to outside air shall have a self-closing device
and have no gaps greater than 1/4 inch when closed. Insect control devices shall be installed so that the devices are not located over a food
or utensil handling areas. Pass-thru windows shall be of an approved size and be equipped with a self-closing device and air curtain if
required. (114259, 114259.2, 114259.3)
