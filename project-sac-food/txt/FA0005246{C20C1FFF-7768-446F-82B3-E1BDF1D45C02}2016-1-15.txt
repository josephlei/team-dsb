# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Facility is currently lacking a Food Safety Certificate. Per employee Rikashni Sandhu, she is currently enrolled in
the course and will be taking her exam in March. Email or fax over proof of enrollment into Food Safety Certification course via
information provided on inspector's business card within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#4

EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE

OBSERVATIONS: -Observed one employee drink with lid and straw stored above food for service. Employee relocated to the lowest
section of refrigeration unit during inspection time.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and
utensils, linens, and single use articles may occur. Employees may drink from closed beverage container if the container is handled to
prevent contamination of the employee's hands, the container, nonprepackaged food, and food-contact surfaces. Area designated for
employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from
contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: -Dust accumulation observed on the fan opposite the dish washer. Clean and maintain fan if it is used to avoid
potential cross contamination.
-Dust accumulation observed on the fan guard inside the three door true refrigerator unit.
Clean and maintain all above locations within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: -Dumpster lids were open during time of inspection. Close immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and
other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 47c

INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY

OBSERVATIONS: -Operator was unable to provide the most current copy of this facility's inspection report. Ensure that the most
current copy of your inspection report is on-site at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be
maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the
most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
