# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed approximately 20 mouse droppings on candy shelf that hold the Starburst candies. All droppings
appear to be an oversight from cleaning the night before. Area was cleaned at the time of inspection.
No evidence of an active vermin infestation was observed at the time of inspection. All surfaces (except as noted above) have been
cleaned and sanitized. Observed approximately 2 dozen glue traps and snap traps placed around perimeter of all rooms within
facility. No activity was observed in any of the traps.
Monitor all traps daily for any activity and remove/replace any traps that do have activity. Routinely use flashlight to look for any
signs of rodent activity within facility and around food storage shelves. Continue to work with pest control company to ensure all
possible entry points have been properly sealed. Make sure front door stays completely closed at all times.
Pest control service is scheduled again for 4/11/16 and then again in 7 days. Fax or email copy of pest control report to inspector
(Attn: S. LePage, lepages@saccounty.net or 916-875-8513). Maintain all pest control reports onsite and available for review by
inspector.
A reinspection will be conducted within 14 days to ensure infestation has been abated.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Found an ice machine with an unapproved drain connection leading outside facility and drain onto floor.
Discontinue use of the ice machine immediately until proper drainage can be achieved. PVC pipe shall be sewer connection with a
minimum of a 1" air gap. Recommend using adjacent mop sink for drainage.
Condensation drain pipes for refrigeration units drain to mop sink without an air gap. Provide a minimum of 1" air gap within 7 days.
2 water supply spigots above mop sink lack backflow prevention. Provide a hose bibb backflow device for each spigot within 7
days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
