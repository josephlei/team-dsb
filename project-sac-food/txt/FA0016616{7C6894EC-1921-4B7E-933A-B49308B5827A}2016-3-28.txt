# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: Hot water measured a maximum 97F at all sinks in the prep and the bar area including handsink, bar 3
compartment sink, food prep sink and food area 3 compartment sink. Employee adjusted water heater. Water measured 103F
towards the end of inspection. Temporarily corrected. Repair water heater to maintain water at 120F within 24 hours. Reinspection
maybe conducted to verify compliance with hot water.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Lack of food managers certification available upon request. Provide within 15 days. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION.
Send proof of correction to fryk@saccounty.net. Failure to comply could result in a reinspection at a fee.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Lack of fixed paper towels at bar hand sink. Provide a fixed paper towel dispenser within 24 hours. 2ND REPEAT
VIOLATION. Send proof of correction to fryk@saccounty.net. Failure to comply could result in a reinspection at a fee.
NOTE: Paper towel dispenser in prep area which is in the general vicinity is used per manager.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: One dead turkestan cockroach observed on the floor in the prep area. Removed at the time of inspection.
Corrected.
Two Turkestan cockroaches observed on glue trap near rear exit. Remove today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Hood filters placed horizontally in ventilation hood. Replace vertically within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
