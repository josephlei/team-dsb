EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: 1. Found (2) Merrychef brand convection microwave ovens in facility. 1 oven installed on prep line behind bar and
1 oven in store room.
Menu includes fries, spring rolls, curry chicken, Kalua pork, meatballs, crusted mahi, coconut shrimp, beef short ribs.
No ventilation hood or fire suppression is installed in facility.
2. Found the following household equipment in facility: crockpot and rice cooker.
Corrective actions:
1. Discontinue preparation and cooking food items not approved by this department. Correct immediately.
2. Remove household equipment or replace with NSF listed, commercial equipment. Correct by 2/24/2016.
Notes:
1. A re-inspection may be conducted to determine compliance. A $268 fee may apply to re-inspection.
2. The following is currently approved by this department for operation in facility:
- 1 NSF listed, commercial, single burner hot plate, maximum 1500watts for boiling only - no grease laden vapor production
approved.
- NSF listed, commercial hot holding equipment.
- bbq operated outside of facility for service of food from inside facility, maintained separate from the public by 5 feet.
3. Prior approval from this department is required before installing additional equipment. Non-compliance may result in
re-inspection and additional fees.
4. No grease residue was observed on wall behind oven or ceiling above oven. Per employee, oven has been operating in facility for
3 months.
