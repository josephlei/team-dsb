#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser at handwashing sink next to chicken prep sink would not dispense. Dispenser was just
stocked and the towels were not completely fed through unit. Corrected at the time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Water temperature at non-adjustable handwashing sink located next to kitchen entrance measured 112F. Provide
water between 100 - 108F within 1 day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 32b

COMPLIANCE WITH TRANS FAT BAN

OBSERVATIONS: Gravy mix contains partially hydrogenated soybean oil and mashed potato mix contains partially hydrogenated
vegetable oil. According to nutritional pamphlet, the menu item KFC Famous Bowls - Mashed Potato with Gravy contains 1 gram
trans fat. Provide nutritional panel for both ingredients that contains trans fat content per serving within 5 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No food containing artificial trans fat may be stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of any food
within a food facility. Every food facility shall maintain on the premises, the label for any food that contains fat, oil, or shortening, for as long
as this food is stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of food within the food facility. Food sold or served in a manufacturer's
original sealed package is exempt from this requirement. (114377)

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Observed leak from condensation drip pan inside chicken walk-in cooler. Speed racks remain relocated away
from condenser unit; no food contamination was observed. Repair within 5 days. Do not store any food items under condenser
until it is repair. Send proof of repair (invoice, work order, etc.) to inspector via fax or e-mail within 3 days, ATTN: S. LePage, (916)
875-8513 or lepages@saccounty.net. Failure to comply may result in a reinspection. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Observed gap between floor and chicken walk-in cooler door of approximately 1-2". Repair within 5 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods
shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and
scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3,
114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Deteriorated grout observed throughout facility, especially around fryers and in chicken walk-in cooler. Repair
within 28 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base coving
shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the juncture
of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268, 114268.1,
114271, 114272)
