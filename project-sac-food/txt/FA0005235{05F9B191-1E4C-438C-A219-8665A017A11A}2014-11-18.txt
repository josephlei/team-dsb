# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: No managers food safety certification available. One person must complete an approved managers food safety
certification class and maintain original certificate on file. Correct within 30 days. REPEAT VIOLATION
Continued non compliance will result in re inspections at a fee of $213.00.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an
employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Measured maximum hot water at hand wash station at 95F and at 3 compartment sink at 115F. Provide hot water at
hand wash station between 100-108F and at three compartment sink at a minimum of 120F. Correct prior to using facility.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot
water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 30a

FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Several boxes of tortilla chips stored on the floor inside dry storage room. Store all food items six inches off the
ground. Correct today.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed
permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged
food may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging.
Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. (114047, 114049, 114053, 114055, 114069 (b))

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Toilet facilities shall be maintained and kept clean. Toilet rooms shall be separated by a well-fitting self-closing
