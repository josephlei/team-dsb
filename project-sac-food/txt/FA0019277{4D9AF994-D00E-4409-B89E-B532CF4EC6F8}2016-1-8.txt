#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS:
MAJOR VIOLATION
Observed Asiago shredded cheese (52F) stored in lexan located inside bakers walk-in cooler. According to employee, item was
recently out of refrigeration. Take smaller portions of item out of refrigeration for use so it does not raise above 41F. Corrected.
MINOR VIOLATION
Milk and half & half located in espresso station lowboy reach-in measured 43-44F. NOTE: Ambient temperature of unit measured
37F.
Small container of spinach & artichoke souffle mix stored in on bottom shelf next to door of bakers walk-in cooler measured 43F.
Relocate storage rack to opposite side of walk-in as discussed to help keep food items 41F/below when door is being opened
frequently.
Sliced ham and turkey measured 43F stored in overfilled containers located in cold-top inserts located in unit next to handwashing
sink. Level of product was reduced to below the fill line. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed approximately 30 drain flies around mop sink and ice machine. Eliminate files immediately. <<REPEAT
VIOLATION>>

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Observed frozen cooked marinated chicken improperly thawing inside large plastic bag inside prep sink. Large
amount of air inside bag did not make it possible for all parts of package to be surrounded by water, nor was there enough running
water around items to ensure it would thaw evenly. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Observed food debris accumulation on shelfs under front counter point of sale machines. Corrected.
Observed ice accumulation on floor of walk-in freezer near door. Correct within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Observed torn gaskets in espresso station lowboy reach-in and in catering cold-top reach-in unit. Replace within
2 weeks.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare
foods shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching
and scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2,
114130.3, 114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed leak at faucet for warewashing sink and handwashing sink across from banquet cold-top unit. Repair
within 7 days.
Hot water handle for handwashing sink across from ice machine is stripped. Repair within 3 days. NOTE: A second handwashing
sink is located on opposite side of wall and is easily accessible.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
