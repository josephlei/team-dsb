Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential
to cause foodborne illness. All major violations must be corrected immediately. Non-compliance may warrant immediate closure of the food facility.

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Corn dogs and hot dogs inside the walk-in refrigerator were all temped at 44 to 45 F. Ambient air temperature near
the walk-in refrigerator door (where these items were stored) was measured at 43 to 44 F. Food items were moved farther inside the
unit, where ambient air temperature was at 39 F, during the inspection.
Prepackaged burritos and sandwiches on the left side of the open merchandiser were measured at 45 to 48 F using a thermocouple.
Sandwiches and burritos on the lower right side of the merchandiser were temped at 41 F. Adjust the thermostat, or if needed, have
the unit serviced within 48 hours to enable it to keep all contents at 41 F or below. The provision of a transparent plastic strip
curtain on the front of the merchandiser was discussed again with the manager.
- Repeat Violation -

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Maximum temperature of hot water at warewash sink was measured at 102 F. Provide hot water at warewash sink
at 120 F minimum at all times. Water heater thermostat was adjusted and hot water at 120 F was available at end of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Accumulation of debris observed on walk-in refrigerator floor, especially under the metro wire racks. Correct
within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Per manager, they use bleach as sanitizer. Available sanitizer test strips are for quaternary ammonia. Provide
chlorine sanitizer test strips within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used.
Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical
warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Rubber gaskets around the walk-in freezer door found in disrepair. Correct within 30 days.

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Probe thermometer was out of calibration. Thermometer calibration procedure was demonstrated during the
inspection.
Monitoring thermometers for the open merchandiser and for the walk-in refrigerator were placed where the cold air comes out.
Code requires monitoring thermometers for refrigeration units to be located at the warmest parts of the units and where they are
easily readable. Corrected during the inspection.
- Repeat Violation -

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
(114157, 114159)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Slime buildup observed inside the floor drain under the 3-compartment sink. Waste water drain line from soda
machine was found with the end inside the floor sink. Clean the floor sink and provide an air gap at least 1 inch above the flood rim
of the floor drain within 2 days.
Waste water drain line from ice machine was found with the end inside the floor sink. Provide an air gap at least 1 inch above the
flood rim of the floor drain within 24 hours.
- THIRD REPEAT VIOLATION - (Observed and written up during the last FOUR routine inspections. Manager stated that they
corrected it but it came loose again. )

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
