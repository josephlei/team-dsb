Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700.
All violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Hot pockets at 50 deg. F, sandwiches at 46 deg. F. Maintain cold potentially hazardous foods at or below 41 deg.
F Correct within 1 day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to
the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Approx. 20 mouse droppings below the soda fountain (in cabinet), about 20 behind the cabinet by bag in the box,
and about 15 in the electrical room, where water is stored. Clean and monitor within 2 days. NOTE: several monitoring sticky traps
are empty.
Note: vermin infestation is grounds for closure.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 32b

COMPLIANCE WITH TRANS FAT BAN

OBSERVATIONS: Donuts lack trans fat information. Provide within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No food containing artificial trans fat may be stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of any food
within a food facility. Every food facility shall maintain on the premises, the label for any food that contains fat, oil, or shortening, for as long
as this food is stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of food within the food facility. Food sold or served in a manufacturer's
original sealed package is exempt from this requirement. (114377)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: 1. Several pieces of domestic equipment, including a microwave in the customer area, double burners, a hand
mixer, etc. in the back. Any domestic (non-ANSI/NSF approved) equipment is for employee use only and must be LABELED or must
be removed and replaced with ANSI/NSF approved equipment. Correct within 7 days.
2. Compressed gas cylinders (by bag in the box) are unsecured. Secure to rigid structure within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Utensils and equipment shall be kept fully operative and in good repair. All new and replacement equipment shall be
properly installed and be certified by NSF, UL Sanitation, or ETL Sanitation. Facilities that wash, rinse, soak, thaw, or similarly prepare foods
shall be provided with a approved food preparation sink. Surfaces such as cutting blocks and boards that are subject to scratching and
scoring shall be resurfaced or replaced if they can no longer be effectively cleaned and sanitized. (114130, 114130.1, 114130.2, 114130.3,
114130.4, 114130.5, 114130.6, 114132, 114133, 114137, 114139, 114153, 114163, 114165, 114169, 114175, 114177, 114180)

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Probe thermometer in the hot pocket/sandwich refrigerator reads 65 deg. F. Provide working thermometer within
7 days.
