HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: House made garlic and oil mixture at 70 F in a squeeze bottle. Per employee, this item had been out approximately
3.5 hours. Garlic in oil is considered a potentially hazardous food and must be kept 41 F or below, or time as a public health control
may be used (a formal system is required - timer, log, marking the container, etc.). Mixture was relocated to prep refrigerator.
Corrected. REPEAT VIOLATION
MINOR VIOLATION
Presets of tomato, onion and lettuce had tomato slices at 44 F in prep line. Lowering the level in the container may help maintain
temperature. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
