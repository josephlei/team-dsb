# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: No managers food safety certification available. One person must complete an approved managers food safety
certification class and maintain original certificate on file. Correct within 30 days. REPEAT VIOLATION
Continued non compliance will result in re inspections at a fee of $213.00.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an
employee who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Both paper towel dispenser were completely out of paper towels. A loose roll of paper towels was available. Paper
towels must always be in dispenser. Correct today.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Measured hot water at three compartment sink stabilizing at 115F. Provide hot water of at least 120F. Correct prior
to using. REPEAT VIOLATION

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot
water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Residue and debris build up observed on both floor sinks. Clean and maintain within 2 days. REPEAT VIOLATION

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and
other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)
