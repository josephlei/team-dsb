#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Half & half inside carafes stored at self-service condiment station measured 54F and 48F. Items and carafes that
were holding them were discarded at the time of inspection.
Half & half stored in carafe located inside left side of 3-door reach-in cooler that will be eventually placed out at condiment station
measured 47F. Ambient temperature on this side of unit measured 37.4F. Milk that was opened by operator in order to take
temperature that is located on the right side of the same unit measured 37F.
NOTE: It appears that facility is correctly following company SOPs regarding this practice. Ensure that the carafes are cold to start
with and that the milk used to fill them measures well below 41F so that items will maintain temperature of 41F/below.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to
the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Hot water at 3-comp ware sink measured 118F. Corrected to 146 F at the time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Shared dumpster left open with lids pinned open between dumpsters and wall. Recommend contacting waste
hauler and requesting lids are closed after they are emptied by driver. Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be kept
in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at all
times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and other
conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)
