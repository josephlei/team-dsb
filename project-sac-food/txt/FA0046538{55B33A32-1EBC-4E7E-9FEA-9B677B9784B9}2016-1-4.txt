COOLING METHODS

MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: MAJORMeasured whole rotisserie chickens inside the walk in cooler which were cooled the night before and measured (temperature
measurement taken at approx. 0815am) between 43-47F. Corrected by voluntarily discarding 14 rotisserie chickens at time of
inspection. (SEE VCD below)

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be rapidly cooled from 135F to 70F within 2 hours, and then from 70F to 41F within
4 hours. Cooling shall be by one or more of the following methods: in shallow containers, separating food into smaller portions, adding ice
as an ingredient, using an ice bath, stirring frequently, using rapid cooling equipment, or using containers that facilitate heat transfer.
(114002, 114002.1)

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Chili soup at the self service area measured between 131-148F. Assistant manager stirred soup to bring it above
135F.
NOTE: Ensure all soups are stirred frequently to ensure proper holding temperatures.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Hot water at three compartment sink measured at 102F maximum. Correct within 48 hours. REPEAT VIOLATION
Send proof of correction (ie. work order, receipt) stating that hot water issue has been corrected to email:
estradam2@saccounty.net. Failure to submit proof of correction will result in a re inspection in 14 days at a fee of $268.00.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 25

PERSONAL CLEANLINESS AND HAIR RESTRAINT

OBSERVATIONS: Observed employee with long facial hair preparing rotisserie chickens. Ensure hair nets are in place when
required. Corrected at time of inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food employees shall wear clean outer clothing to prevent contamination of food, equipment, utensils, linens, and
single-use articles. Employees preparing, serving or handling food or utensils shall wear hair restraints such as hats, hair coverings, or nets.
Food employees shall keep their fingernails trimmed, filed, and maintained or shall wear gloves when contacting food and food-contact
surfaces. (113968, 113969, 113971, 113973)
