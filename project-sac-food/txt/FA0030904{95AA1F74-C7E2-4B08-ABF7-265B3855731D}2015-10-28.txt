# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: No air gap available at warewash sink. Keep drain pipe at least one inch above floor drain. Correct within 48
hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)

# 49

PERMIT REQUIREMENTS

OBSERVATIONS: Facility is selling chili from a hot holding dispenser and selling self service donuts. A hot dog roller is also on
counter, but not being used at time of inspection. Only prepacked food is allowed with current permit.
Remove hot dog warmer, donuts and chili dispenser immediately or apply with EMD for a permit upgrade. Provide proof of
correction to EMD within 72 hours in order to avoid reinspection and fee.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall not be open for business without a valid permit. Permit shall be posted in a conspicuous place
within the food facility. (114067(b, c), 114381, 114387)
