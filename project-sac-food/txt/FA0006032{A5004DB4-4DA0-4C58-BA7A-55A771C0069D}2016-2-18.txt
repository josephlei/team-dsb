#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Sliced tomatoes at the salad bar above 51 deg. F. These are the only potentially hazardous food (phf) served at
the salad bar. The tomatoes were full and appeared to be just placed out on the line.
This product is put out at 10:30, then cafe service is stopped at 2:00. You can use time as a public health control IF you discard any
phf after 4 hours/close of lunch. If using time as a public health control, you cannot use temperature too.
OR maintain cold at or below 41 deg. F if tomatoes will be used again/after the 4 hour time period. Time as a public health control
requires tracking: a policy/procedure or other means to show that this product is discarded after 4 hours and not re-served.
FYI, salad bar phf can be held at 45 deg. F if discarded after 12 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to
the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
