# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: THIRD NOTICE: Hot water today at 115F at 3 comp sink and prep sink. These sinks must have hot water at a
minimum of 120F available at all times. Provide documentation of correction within 2 days: talusikj@saccounty.net

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Inside of drawers and shelving surfaces noted with some debris accumulation and sticky liner that is peeling.
Some surfaces have chipping paint. Clean Shelves and drawers thoroughly and provide durable cleanable surface. Shelving and
drawers may be lined with Fiberglass Reinforced Paneling. Correct 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Water bottle noted next to cooking equipment. Keep personal beverages separate and away from prep area. If
drinking containers will be kept in kitchen, avoid using screw top bottles - recommend using cup and straw. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers
or other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)
