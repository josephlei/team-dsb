Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Whole milk found inside the reach-in refrigerator at the cold beverage station (CBS) was measured at 47 F using a
thermocouple. Do not leave potentially hazardous food (PHF) items out of temperature holding longer than necessary. Ambient air
temperature inside the refrigerator was measured at 40 F. Manager adjusted the unit's thermostat during the inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 30a

FOOD STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Cartons of coffee and to-go containers were found stored directly on the floor in the back dry storage area. Food
and food contact items shall be stored at least 6 inches above the floor. Corrected during the inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food items shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in an approved location within the fully enclosed
permitted food facility. Food shall not be exposed to splash, dust, vermin, or other forms of contamination or adulteration. Prepackaged
food may not be stored in direct contact with ice if the food is subject to the entry of water because of the nature of it's packaging.
Non-prepackaged food may not be stored in direct contact with undrained ice. (114047, 114049, 114053, 114055, 114069 (b))

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Dust accumulation observed on ceiling vents and ducts above cold beverage station and warewash area (pointed
out to manager). Correct within 2 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Monitoring thermometer in reach-in refrigerator under the front counter was defective. Provide functioning
thermometer and situate at the warmest part of the unit and where it is easily readable. Replaced with a new thermometer during the
inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
(114157, 114159)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Shared garbage dumpster outside was found with lids open. Work with co-tenants and/or landlord to always keep
lids closed when not being filled to prevent vermin infestation and possible dirty water (leachate) run-off to storm water drain. Close
garbage dumpster lids as soon as possible today.
- Repeat Violation -

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and
other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)
