#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Chimichangas (124F) and corn dogs (114F) that were cooked at approximately 9 am, stored in counter top hot
holding unit were out of proper holding temperature. Items were reheated in deep fryer. Unit thermostat was raised at the time of
inspection. Ensure items remain at/above 135F at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed approximately 60-80 old mouse droppings along perimeter of wall inside back storage room with fryer
and approximately 30 old mouse droppings along perimeter of wall in storage room with ice machine. Facility uses Advantage Pest
control for routine service. Provide additional service and clean all droppings within 24 hours.
Seal all gaps and cracks along perimeter of wall within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Observed grease accumulation under fryer in back room. Facility is also using unapproved carpet mat for directly
in front of unit. remove carpet and clean underneath unit within 3 days. NOTE: Fryer is a Crispy Lite unit, model #CV15EFL. Unit has
a built-in hood filter and Ansul system. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 34

WAREWASHING FACILITIES AND TESTING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Facility lacks chlorine sanitizer test strips. Provide within 3 days. Handout provided.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Facilities that prepare food shall be equipped with a manual warewashing sink that has at least three compartments
with two integral metal drainboards. A two compartment sink that was installed before January 1, 1996, need not be replaced when used in
approved manner. Sink compartments shall be large enough to accommodate immersion of the largest equipment and utensils used.
Testing materials shall be provided to adequately measure the applicable sanitization method used during manual or mechanical
warewashing. (113984(e), 114067(f,g), 114099, 114099.3, 114099.5, 114101(a), 114101.1, 114101.2, 114103, 114107, 114125)

# 35

EQUIPMENT APPROVED AND MAINTAINED

OBSERVATIONS: Observed ice build up inside ice cream chest freezer. Clean within 7 days.

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Facility lacks metal probe thermometer to measure internal temperatures. Provide a thermometer that reads from
0 - 220F within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
(114157, 114159)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Observed damaged floor inside room with ice machine. Repair within 30 days. <<REPEAT VIOLATION>>
Observed several gaps and cracks around perimeter of floor and base boards inside storage room with fryer. Seal all within 7 days.
Observed deteriorated floor with holes behind file cabinet next to desk in back room. Repair floor within 30 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
