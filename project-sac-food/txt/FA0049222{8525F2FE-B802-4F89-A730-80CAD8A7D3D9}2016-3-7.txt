Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: MAJOR VIOLATION - Two cartons of liquid egg whites (64F) placed back in the reach-in outside of the office were
out of proper holding temperature. Maintain all potentially hazardous foods (PHF) at proper holding temperature at all times. These
items were voluntarily discarded.
MINOR VIOLATION - Liquid eggs placed back in the reach-in outside of the office was out of proper holding temperature. Maintain
PHF at proper holding temperature. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

HANDWASHING PROCEDURES

OBSERVATIONS: One employee waiting for food to be made coughed on her hands but failed to wash hands immediately after. All
hands must be adequately washed after coming in contact with unclean surfaces or becomes contaminated by sneeze or cough.
Implement proper practice immediately. Employee was requested to wash hands immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees are required to wash their hands in the following situations: before engaging in food preparation, after
touching bare skin, after using the toilet room, after coughing or sneezing, after eating, drinking, or using tobacco, after handling soiled
equipment or utensils, as often as necessary during food preparation to prevent cross-contamination, when switching from working with raw
to ready to eat foods, before donning gloves, before dispensing or serving food, after engaging in other activities that may contaminate
hands. (113952, 113953.3, 113953.4)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Soap dispenser by the office was partially blocked by a spray bottle of cleaner. Do not place any item in front of or
around the handwash station or its supplies that may impede proper handwashing. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Water dispensed from the women's restroom handwash station was not adequate at 67F. Repair to dispense
water at 100-108F at all times. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 27

FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Cartons of liquid eggs/egg whites are stored at room temperature without any temperature or time control.
Implement a system that maintains these items safe to consume. Implement immediately.
Open bag of sugar stored on the counter across the front cash registers. Store all open packages of food items in containers with a
tight fitting lid. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food
and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze
guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially
hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from
public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Floor under the deep fryers (near the office) and under the beverage station is in need of cleaning. Clean floor
within 48 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 39

TEMPERATURE MEASURING DEVICES

OBSERVATIONS: Thermometer was not functional inside the reach-in below the hot holding units. Replaced at the time of
inspection.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An accurate easily readable metal probe thermometer suitable for measuring temperature of food shall be available
to the food handler. Thermometers shall be calibrated in accordance with manufacturer's specifications as necessary to ensure their
accuracy. A thermometer shall be provided for all refrigerators, hot and cold holding units, and high temperature warewashing machines.
(114157, 114159)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Men's restroom toilet paper dispenser was empty. Roll of toilet paper was stored on the handicap support bar.
Keep dispenser adequately filled at all times. Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and
other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 44e

VERMIN AND ANIMAL EXCLUSION

OBSERVATIONS: Sanitizer by the flat grill in the cook's line was observed at 0ppm chlorine. Maintain sanitizer solution at least
100ppm chlorine. Correct immediately.
One wet wiping cloth was stored on the prep sink. Store all wet wiping cloths in sanitizer at all times. Correct immediately.
