#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Major violations:
- Observed an over-portioned pan of cooked pork (114-120f) inside of steam table. Do not store any potentially hazardous foods
(PHFs) above load fill line inside of individual pans. Corrected on site.
- Bag of pork tamales (124-126f) inside of microwave unit. Maintain all PHFs at/below 41f or at/above 135f at all times. Corrected on
site, as cook told specialist that he had placed inside of microwave unit to reheat within the past 25 minutes but forgot to check on
them. The tamales were allowed to be reheated to 165f and placed into tamale steam table line.
Minor violation:
Observed a pan of over-portioned amount of shredded jack cheese (47f) inside of cold top line. Do not store any PHFs above load
fill line. Corrected on site.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)
