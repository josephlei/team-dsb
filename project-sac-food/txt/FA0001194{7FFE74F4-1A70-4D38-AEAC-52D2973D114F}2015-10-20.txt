Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Warm water in restroom only reached 81F. A minimum of 100F must be readily available for handwashing.
Correct within 24 hours. If water cannot be readily available for handwashing, an insta-hot unit will need to be installed to provide
warm water for handwashing.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 30b

FOOD CONTAINERS LABELED

OBSERVATIONS: Bulk barrels lack required labels. Bulk FOOD that is available for CONSUMER self-service shall be prominently
labeled with either of the following in plain view of the CONSUMER:
(1) The manufacturer's or processor's label that was provided with the FOOD.
(2) A card, sign, or other method of notification that includes the information specified under paragraphs (1), (2), and (5) of
subdivision (b).

Pre-packaged self-service candy lacks required labels. Each package must have a label that has name and address of
manufacturer, list of ingredients, and common name of food.
Provide all labels as required within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Containers holding food or food ingredients that are removed from their original packaging shall be identified with the
common name of the food. (114051)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Grime on door inside storage room that leads to retail floor. Clean today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 41

PLUMBING FIXTURES AND BACKFLOW PREVENTION

OBSERVATIONS: Sink in restroom is slow draining. Clear within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Steam tables, ice machines, ice bins, food preparation sinks, display cases, walk-in refrigeration units, and other
similar equipment that discharge liquid waste shall drain into a floor sink with an air gap or other approved type of receptor. Plumbing and
plumbing fixtures shall be maintained so as to prevent contamination, shall be kept clean, fully operative, and in good repair. The potable
water supply shall be protected with a backflow or back siphonage protection device. Any hose used for potable water shall be of approved
materials, labeled, properly stored, and used for no other purpose. (114171, 114190, 114192, 114193, 114193.1, 114199, 114201, 114269)
