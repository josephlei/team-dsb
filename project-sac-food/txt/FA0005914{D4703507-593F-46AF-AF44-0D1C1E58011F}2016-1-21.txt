Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: The following food items are in violation of proper holding temperatures: cut ham (55F), sausages (70F), ground
beef (57F), sliced tomatoes (55F), and shell eggs (60F). All items are stored on the counter at room temperature. Relocated to reach
in cooler at the time of inspection. 3RD REPEAT VIOLATION.
Facility has an option of using time as a public health control. Keeping log and usage of time control have been discussed today.
RE-INSPECTION IS REQUIRED TO FOLLOW UP COMPLIANCE SINCE THIS IS A 3RD REPEAT VIOLATION. ALL POTENTIALLY
HAZARDOUS FOODS MUST BE KEPT AT/ABOVE 135F OR AT/ BELOW 41F AT ALL TIMES.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Food safety certificates have been expired. One person must renew within 60 days and fax copy/ evidence of
passing exam.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Kitchen area hand wash station is completely blocked. Operator stated they use 3 compartment sink to wash
hands. Soap and paper towels available by 3 compartment sink. All hand wash stations must be fully accessible and supplied at all
times. Corrected. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing
areas. Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap
and sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair.
(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)

# 14a

FOOD CONTACT SURFACES CLEANED AND SANITIZED

OBSERVATIONS: - Meat slicer is unclean and accumulated with grease/ food debris. Take it apart, wash, rinse, and sanitize inside 3
compartment sink. Correct today .
- Interior ceilings of ice machine is accumulated with slime/ mold. Remove all ice, wash, rinse, and sanitize within one day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment shall be cleaned and sanitized at the following times: before each
use with different type of raw food of animal origin, when changing from raw food to ready to eat food, between uses with raw produce and
potentially hazardous food, before using a thermometer, any time during the operation when contamination may have occurred.
Food-contact surfaces and utensils shall be clean to sight and touch. (114113, 114115, 114117, 114125(b), 114141)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Observed frozen meat improperly thawing on the counter. Must use one of approved methods (immersed under
cold running water, during cooking process, or overnight inside refrigeration unit). Correct immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: - Freezer door handles inside back room prep area are accumulated with food debris. Clean and sanitize within
one day.
- Floors through out kitchen are unclean and in need of proper cleaning. Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: - Tallow bin area is accumulated with grease and trash. Clean up the trash and collect grease using kitty litter.
Correct within 7 days.
- Community shared dumpster lids are left open when not in use. Keep it closed at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be
kept in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at
all times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and
other conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: - Cove base tiles through out kitchen area are deteriorated/ detached. Repair within 14 days.
- Floor tiles are missing in front of ice machine and at the front kitchen entrance. Replace within 30 days. REPEAT VIOLATION.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
