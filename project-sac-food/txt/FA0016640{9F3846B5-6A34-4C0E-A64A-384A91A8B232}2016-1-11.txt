Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700.
All violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the

#8

TIME AS A PUBLIC HEALTH CONTROL

OBSERVATIONS: Times not logged on Time Control Sheet for several food items at time of inspection. Per Staff, it was too busy.
Maintain at all times. Small amount of food was observed in self serve and was replaced with new food products. Time for new
items logged.

CODE DESCRIPTION: When time as a public health control is used, the following procedures shall be observed: food items shall be marked
to indicate four hours past the time when the food was removed from temperature control, food shall be served or discarded within the
four-hour time limit, written procedures for food that was prepared, cooked, and refrigerated before time as a public health control was used,
shall be maintained at the food facility and be made available to the enforcement agency upon request. (114000)

# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Manager's Food Safety Certificate is not available for review. Correct within 60 days.
NOTE: Maintain on-site for review.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: 1 of 2 paper towel dispensers in catering kitchen has been detached from wall and also out of batteries. Catering
kitchen is currently not in use at time of inspection. Correct immediately.
NOTE: Provide proof of corrections within 3 days to MOUAST@SACCOUNTY.NET. Non compliance will result in
reinspections/fees.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Peeled hard boiled eggs in self serve merchandiser measured at 46F. Unit is ambient at 47F and observed with
ice build-up on compressor like unit in interior compartment. Repair/replace unit immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return to
the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: No hot water at catering kitchen hand sink nearest food preparation tables. Catering kitchen is currently not in
use at of inspection. Correct immediately.
NOTE: Provide proof of corrections within 3 days to MOUAST@SACCOUNTY.NET. Non compliance will result in
reinspections/fees.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Unlabeled employee use microwaves observed in both kitchens. Label as personal/employee use immediately.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers or
other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)

PLACARD POSTED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: GREEN PLACARD staged in a frame was observed in kitchen and not in plain sight of patrons at time of

times. Placard will be placed at front door until arrival of new frame.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Upon completion of a routine inspection or re-inspection, the Health Officer shall post at every food establishment the
appropriate placard, as determined by the Health Officer. The placard shall be posted so as to be clearly visible to the general public and to
patrons entering the food establishment. (Sacramento County Code 6.04.021)
