Items listed on this report as violations do not meet the requirements set forth in the California Health and Safety Code commencing with section 7; 113700. All
violations must be corrected within specified timeframe. Violations that are classified as "Major" pose an immediate threat to public health and have the potential
to cause foodborne illness. All major violations must be corrected immediately. Non-compliance may warrant immediate closure of the food facility.

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Warm water at only handsink observed starting at 78F and only rising to 96F. REPEAT VIOLATION. Warm water at
pre-mixed valves must be maintained between 100-108F. An insta-hot unit may need to be installed. Correct within 48 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 25

PERSONAL CLEANLINESS AND HAIR RESTRAINT

OBSERVATIONS: Employee observed with long unrestrained hair. Hair should be adequately restrained to prevent the employee
from needing to
contact or adjust it with their hands during work.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food employees shall wear clean outer clothing to prevent contamination of food, equipment, utensils, linens, and
single-use articles. Employees preparing, serving or handling food or utensils shall wear hair restraints such as hats, hair coverings, or nets.
Food employees shall keep their fingernails trimmed, filed, and maintained or shall wear gloves when contacting food and food-contact
surfaces. (113968, 113969, 113971, 113973)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Dust accumulation in ceiling vent above back door leading to restrooms. Clean within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 44d

EMPLOYEE PERSONAL ITEMS

OBSERVATIONS: Several employee beverages (in approved containers) stored above 3-comp sink. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION. Store
personal belongings below/away from food or related equipment at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No person shall store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage and preparation of food. Lockers
or other suitable facilities shall be provided and used for the orderly storage of employee clothing and other possessions. Lockers or other
suitable facilities shall be located in a designated room or area where contamination of food, equipment, utensils, and single-use articles
cannot occur. (114256, 114256.1.)
