# 1a

DEMONSTRATION OF KNOWLEDGE

OBSERVATIONS: Employee not aware of proper thawing method. Provided education.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food employees shall have adequate knowledge of food safety as it relates to their assigned duties. (113947)

# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Food safety certificate not available. 4th Repeat violation. A charged reinspection of $227 will be applied for each
reinspection to follow up on compliance.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Observed frozen soft serve cartons of yogurt mix being thawed in sanitize water at 93F in the 3 compartment sink.
Thaw using the refrigerator or in cold water water. Corrected. Item were removed, washed, and placed into the cooler.
Do not allow product to be at or above 41F during the thawing process.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)
