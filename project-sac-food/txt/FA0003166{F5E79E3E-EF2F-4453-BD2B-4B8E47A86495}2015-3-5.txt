REHEATING PROCEDURES FOR HOT HOLDING

OBSERVATIONS: Facility using mobile hot plate to heat beef and chicken to 135F. Heating potentially hazardous foods are
prohibited on satellite food facility. All potentially hazardous foods can be reheated in fixed food facility. Only hot holding at 135F
or above can occur on satellite unit.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods that are cooked, cooled, and subsequently reheated for hot holding or service, shall
be heated to a temperature of 165F for 15 seconds. Ready-to-eat food taken from a commercailly processed hermetically sealed container, or
from an intact package from a food processing plant shall be heated to a temperature of 135F for hot holding. (114016)
