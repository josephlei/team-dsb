VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

MAJOR / CLOSURE

OBSERVATIONS: Observed cockroaches at the following locations:
- more than 10 live cockroaches behind spring roll prep refrigerator
- 1 baby nymph walking outside along the walls of the walk-in refrigerator
- 1 dead adult cockroach on the walls of upright refrigerator next to wok station
- 2 adult cockroaches crawling beneath the prep table shelf near back exit door
- 1 cockroach inside glue traps at back exit door
- 3 cockroaches crawling beneath the dessert refrigerator
- more than 5 live cockroaches behind ice machine
- more than 5 dead cockroaches on glue traps behind ice machine
Remove bodies immediately and clean/sanitize area.

Due to cockroach infestation, facility is CLOSED and health permit is temporarily suspended. Facility is prohibited from preparing
and selling food.
The following must be completed before a re-inspection can be conducted:
1. Contact a pest control service for immediate consultation/service. Keep receipt of service.
2. Seal all gaps, holes, and cracks of facility.
3. Remove all dead cockroaches.
4. Thoroughly clean and sanitize any areas that may have become contaminated by the infestation.
5. Thoroughly clean any areas that have food debris, dirt, grease buildup, or clutter which may contribute to the harborage and
infestation or vermin.
When facility is FREE of cockroach infestation and the aforementioned items completed call Sothea Chhang at (916) 875-8463 for a
re-inspection.
