#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: Spicy chicken wings at 118-142F. Regular wings at 114-147F. Ambient temperature was set to 150F. There are
cooked in turbo oven and placed inside hot holding case. Door to case was closed at start of inspection. The ones low in
temperature are near the door opening. Six chicken wings were reheated to 165F and placed back into hot case.
Vanilla creamer containing whole milk is at 58F inside dispenser. The dispenser had an error message and had condensation on the
inside of dispenser. Thermometer inside unit was reading 60F. Boxes of creamers were discarded and service call was placed. Do
not use unit until repair has been made and unit is able to maintain temperature at/below 41F.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: -Paper towel dispenser not available. It has broken and a new one is on order inside employee restroom. A roll of
paper towels is available.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing

(113953, 113953.1, 113953.2)
