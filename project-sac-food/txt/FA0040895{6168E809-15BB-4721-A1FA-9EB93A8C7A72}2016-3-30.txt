# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

MAJOR

OBSERVATIONS: Maximum water temperature at hand sink and 3 compartment sink at 70 F. This snack bar is not in active use at
this time. Provide proof of correction or be subject to reinspection at cost. Hand sink must have warm water between 100-108 F. 3
compartment sink must have hot water at 120 F. Correct prior to next use.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Food safety certificate not available at this snack bar. Snack bar is not related to cafeteria. If open potentially
hazardous foods are served in this snack bar, a food safety manager certificate is required. Correct prior to next use.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

# 23

VERMIN AND ANIMAL CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: ~6-7 rodent droppings under counter on field side of snack bar. Droppings do not appear to be new. Clean this
area thoroughly and monitor for new activity.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A food facility shall at all times be equipped, maintained, and operated as to prevent the entrance and harborage of
animals, birds, and vermin, including, but not limited to rodents and insects. (114259, 114259.1, 114259.4, 114259.5)

# 47b

PLACARD POSTED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Placard was in reach in chest freezer. Placard must be posted visibly. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Upon completion of a routine inspection or re-inspection, the Health Officer shall post at every food establishment
the appropriate placard, as determined by the Health Officer. The placard shall be posted so as to be clearly visible to the general public
and to patrons entering the food establishment. (Sacramento County Code 6.04.021)

# 47c

INSPECTION REPORT AVAILABILITY

OBSERVATIONS: Most recent inspection report not available in snack bar. Maintain a copy of the most recent inspection report
available.

CODE DESCRIPTION: A copy of the most recent routine inspection report conducted to assess compliance with this part shall be
maintained at the food facility and made available upon request. The food facility shall post a notice advising consumers that a copy of the
most recent routine inspection report is available for review by any interested party. (113725.1)
