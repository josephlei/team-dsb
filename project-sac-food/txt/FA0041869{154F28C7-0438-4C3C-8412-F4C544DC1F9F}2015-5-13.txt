# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: Ware washing sink and handsink measured 71F. Per janitor, the sink hot water are turned off during off season.
Kitchen was not in use at the time of inspection. Turn on hot water before operation and ensure ware sink faucet reaches 120F.
Correct before operation.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot
water shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Continental refrigerator base unclean with food debris. Clean and sanitize before the start of season.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and
other debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 36

EQUIPMENT STORED IN APPROVED LOCATION

OBSERVATIONS: Several cookie sheets were stored on the floor against warming unit. Relocate 6 inches off the floor. Rewash,
rinse, and sanitize before use. Relocate off the floor at the start of season.

CALCODE DESCRIPTION: Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall be stored at least six inches above the floor in
an approved location within the fully enclosed permitted food facility. Clean equipment, utensils, linens, and single-use articles shall not be
exposed to splash, dust, vermin or other forms of contamination. Non-food items shall be stored and displayed separate from food and
food-contact surfaces. During pauses in food preparation utensils shall be stored with handles above the top of the food, on a clean
sanitized surface, in running water, or in water that is at least 135F. Single-use articles and multiservice utensils shall be handled,
displayed, and dispensed so that contamination of food and lip-contact surfaces is prevented. Pressurized cylinders shall be securely
fastened to rigid structure. (114074, 114081, 114119, 114121, 114161, 114172, 114178, 114179, 114083, 114185, 114185.2, 114185.3,
114185.4, 114185.5)
