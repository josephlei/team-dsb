#6

HANDWASHING FACILITIES

OBSERVATIONS: Paper towel dispenser in 1 of 2 restrooms in warehouse observed with a loose roll. Maintain in dispenser at all
times. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Handwashing facilities shall be provided within or adjacent to toilet rooms, food preparation, and warewashing areas.
Handwashing facilities shall be clean, unobstructed, and accessible at all times. Handwashing facilities shall be provided with soap and
sanitary single-use towels or heated-air hand drying device in approved dispensers. Dispensers shall be maintained in good repair. (113953,
113953.1, 113953.2)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Areas observed with old food/grease/residue like accumulation:
-dairy walk in cooler floor 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION
-interior base of shelvings in self serve merchandiser for packaged chicken/cornish game hens 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION
-island freezer that stages seafood
-produce walk in floor under wooden pallet leading into warehouse
-floor around baler
-exterior sections of meat slicer in meat department
-exterior and hard to reach areas of shrink wrap machines
-walk in freezer floor
Correct within 3 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 42

GARBAGE AND REFUSE DISPOSAL

OBSERVATIONS: Dumpster enclosure observed with trash debris/litter. Correct within 24 hours. 2ND REPEAT VIOLATION
Lids on dumpsters are open. Keep closed when not in use. Correct today.

CODE DESCRIPTION: The exterior premises of a food facility shall be kept free of litter and rubbish. All food waste and rubbish shall be kept
in nonabsorbent, durable, cleanable, leak-proof, and rodent-proof containers. Containers shall have close-fitting lids and be covered at all
times. All waste must be removed and disposed of as frequently as necessary to prevent the development of objectionable odors and other
conditions that attract or harbor insects or rodents. (114244, 114245, 114245.1, 114245.2, 114245.3, 114245.4, 114245.5, 114245.6,
114245.7, 114257)

# 43

RESTROOMS MAINTAINED, SUPPLIED, AND ACCESSIBLE

OBSERVATIONS: Door handle to 1 of 2 restroom in the warehouse is damaged. Repair within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Toilet facilities shall be maintained and kept clean. Toilet rooms shall be separated by a well-fitting self-closing door.
Toilet tissue shall be provided in a permanently installed dispenser at each toilet. The number of toilet facilities shall be in accordance with
local building and plumbing ordinances. Toilet facilities shall be provided for patrons: in establishments with more than 20,000 sq. ft.;
establishments offering on-site consumption of food. (114250, 114250.1, 114276)
