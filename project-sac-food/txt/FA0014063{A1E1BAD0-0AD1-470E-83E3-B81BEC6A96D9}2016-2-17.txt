#7

HOT AND COLD HOLDING TEMPERATURES

OBSERVATIONS: - Whipped butter 72F and garlic with whipped butter 84F was stored on top shelf at cook's line. Employee
voluntarily discarded during inspection.
- Steak 56F, corn beef, 53F, potato salad 53F, ham 51F, sliced turkey 55F, roast beef 49F, sliced cheeses 47F-49F stored inside cold
top cooler across from pizza oven. Also, raw chicken 51F and sliced cheese 51F inside reach-in cooler. Ambient of reach-in cooler
was 57F during inspection (per facilities monitoring thermometer 52F). Food from cold top cooler was voluntarily discarded and food
from reach-in cooler was moved to walk-in cooler. (Also, see violation #35) Food items that could be saved were placed inside ice
bath.
Minor Violation
- Several containers with various sliced and shredded cheese, milk, pooled eggs, buttermilk, and steak 43F-44F inside walk-in
cooler. Ambient temperature inside walk-in cooler was 44F. (Also, see violation #35)
Potentially hazardous foods may not be stored at room temperature unless time as a public health control (TPHC) is used. Store
foods at/below 41F or at/above 135F. TPHC handout provided & discussed with operator. Highly recommend having temperature log
for all coolers and have employees very temperature several times a day.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Potentially hazardous foods shall be maintained at or below 41F or at or above 135F. The following foods may be
held at or below 45F: raw shell eggs, unshucked live molluscan shellfish, pasteurized milk and pasteurized milk products in original sealed
containers, potentially hazardous foods held during transportation. Food preparation shall not exceed two cumulative hours without a return
to the required holding temperatures. (113996, 113998, 114037)

EATING, DRINKING, OR TOBACCO USE

OBSERVATIONS: Found open cup of employee beverage stored inside reach-in cooler next to clean plates (cooler located across
from pizza oven). All employee beverages must be stored in a cup with straw and lid and located away/below from food and food
equipment. Corrected. REPEAT VIOLATION

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not eat, drink, or use tobacco in any area where contamination of food, clean equipment and

employees to eat and drink shall be located so that food, clean equipment and utensils, linens, and single-use articles are protected from
contamination. (113977, 114256 (a))

# 21

HOT AND COLD WATER SUPPLY

OBSERVATIONS: - Hot water temperature at kitchen waresink measured at 115F, then dropped to 102F then went up to 112F. Water
temperature fluctuates. Also, hot water temperature fluctuates at bar warewash sink: measured temperature 115F, dropped to 105F
and then went up to 118F. Inspect water heater especially heating elements for any leak and ensure at least 120F of hot water
provided to warewash sink at all times. Correct today.
***Continue non-compliance may result in re-inspection chargeable $268.00 or compliance conference for owners/managers in EMD
office with a fee of $402. 4th REPEAT VIOLATION
- Faucet handle was missing for hot water at bar handwash station. Operator provided loose handle but was unable to secure it
properly. Correct within 24 hours.

CODE DESCRIPTION: An adequate, protected, pressurized, potable supply of hot and cold water shall be provided at all times. Hot water
shall be supplied at a minimum temperature of at least 120F when measured from the faucet. The temperature of water provided to
handwashing facilities shall be at least 100F. Handwashing facilities equipped with auto run faucets shall have their water temperature set
between 100F to 108F. (113953(c), 114101(d), 114189, 114192, 114192.1, 114195)

# 26

THAWING METHODS

OBSERVATIONS: Sliced meat improperly thawing on the counter top. Education and thawing sticker was provided. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Frozen foods shall be stored or displayed in their frozen state unless being thawed in the following manner: under
refrigeration, completely submerged under cold running water with sufficient velocity to flush loose particles, in microwave oven, during the
cooking process. (114018, 114020)

# 27

FOOD PROTECTED FROM CONTAMINATION

OBSERVATIONS: Observed ice for consumption in direct contact with soda gun tubes inside cooling bin at bar area. Ice for
consumption must be protected from contamination at all times. Correct in approved method within 7 days. Temporarily ice will be
placed inside a tub.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food shall be protected from contamination during transportation, storage, preparation, holding, and display. Food
and condiments on display shall be protected from contamination by the use of dispensers, packaging, counter, service line, or sneeze
guards that intercept a direct line between the consumer's mouth and the food being displayed. Only prepackaged non-potentially
hazardous food or uncut produce may be displayed or sold outdoors if under overhead cover. Open-air barbecues shall be separated from
public access. (113984 (a-f), 113986, 114060, 114067, 114069, 114077, 114089.1 (c), 114143 (c-e))

# 44b

APPROPRIATE USE OF EQUIPMENT

OBSERVATIONS: Observed employee washing hands at kitchen warewash sink. All equipment must be properly used. Use
designated handwash sink for handwashing. Corrected.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Employees shall not clean their hands in a sink used for food preparation, warewashing, or in a service sink or a
curbed cleaning facility used for the disposal of mop water and similar liquid waste. A handwashing facility shall not be used for purposes
other than handwashing. Food preparation sinks, handwashing lavatories, and warewashing equipment shall not be used for the cleaning
of maintenance tools, the preparation or holding of maintenance materials, or the disposal of mop water and similar liquid wastes. Open-air
barbecue or outdoor wood-burning oven shall be operated on the same premises as, in reasonable proximity to, and in conjunction with, a
permanent food facility that is approved for food preparation. (113953.1 (b-d), 114123, 114143 (a,b), Exception 114125)

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: - Hole/gaps observed: in metal wall below & above steam table, near CO2 tanks located by walk-in cooler, on
ceiling above clean pots storage area, between rubber coving in multiple areas (bar and warewash/handwash area). Seal all
holes/gaps with approved material within 30 days. REPEAT VIOLATION
- Gap on ceiling between ceiling panels at storage room storing ice machine. Also, broken attic cover that is located above
tea/coffee dispenser. Correct in approved matter within 21 days. REPEAT VIOLATION
- Uncleanable flooring (exposed concrete) and floor paint deteriorating at kitchen area in multiple areas. Also, missing coving
beneath kitchen warewash sink. Provide approved flooring and coving. Flooring handout was provided.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
