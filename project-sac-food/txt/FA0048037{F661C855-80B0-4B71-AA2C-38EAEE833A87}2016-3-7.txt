# 1b

FOOD SAFETY CERTIFICATION

OBSERVATIONS: Lack of food safety manager certificate. Per person in charge, Vilash Roma 3/3/21 is currently using same
certificate at Rocklin (Placer County) store. Food safety manager certificate cannot be used in multiple locations. Person in charge
notified inspector that they will have another manager certificate after March 16, 2016. Correct within 60 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities that prepare, handle, or serve non-prepackaged potentially hazardous food, shall have an employee
who has passed an approved food safety certification examination. (113947.1)

# 32b

COMPLIANCE WITH TRANS FAT BAN

OBSERVATIONS: - Unable to review trans fat information for all ice creams since ingredients are not really legible on ice cream lid
containers.
- Missing trans fat information for multi star quins glazed 9496 that contain partially hydrogenated oil as an ingredient.
Panels must be available to verify that all non-prepackaged food items are trans-fat free. Provide required information within 30
days and maintain copies on-site at all times.

CODE DESCRIPTION: No food containing artificial trans fat may be stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of any food
within a food facility. Every food facility shall maintain on the premises, the label for any food that contains fat, oil, or shortening, for as long
as this food is stored, distributed, served by, or used in the preparation of food within the food facility. Food sold or served in a
manufacturer's original sealed package is exempt from this requirement. (114377)

# 33

NONFOOD-CONTACT SURFACES CLEAN

OBSERVATIONS: Ice build up inside condenser unit at walk-in freezer. Remove all ice and investigate leak. Repair if needed.
Correct within 7 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other
debris. Interior premises of a food facility shall be kept clean. (114115 (b), 114257)

# 45

FLOOR, WALL, AND CEILING FINISHES

OBSERVATIONS: Missing ceiling panel near rear back door. Install approved ceiling panel within 14 days.

CODE DESCRIPTION: Food facilities shall be fully enclosed in a building consisting of permanent floors, walls, and an overhead structure
that meets minimum standards. The walls and ceiling shall be smooth, durable, nonabsorbent, and easily cleanable. Flooring and base
coving shall be smooth, durable, and made of approved nonabsorbent material that is easily cleanable. Floor surfaces shall be coved at the
juncture of the floor and wall with a 3/8 inch minimum radius and shall extend up the wall at least 4 inches. (114143 (d), 114266, 114268,
114268.1, 114271, 114272)
